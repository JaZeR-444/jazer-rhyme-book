# WORD: Aedicula

## Meaning (plain):
A small shrine; in classical architecture, a decorative frame, usually consisting of two columns supporting an entablature and a pediment, surrounding a niche, door, or window.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a personal space of worship or a highly structured, ornate "frame" for one's persona or talent. Represents a small but sacred or significant monument.
