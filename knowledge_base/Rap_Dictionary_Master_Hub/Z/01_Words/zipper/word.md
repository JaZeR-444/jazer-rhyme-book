# WORD: Zipper

## Meaning (plain):
A fastener consisting of two rows of metal or plastic teeth drawn together by a sliding piece.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing "shutting mouths," closing the case, or having that "sliding" smooth movement.
