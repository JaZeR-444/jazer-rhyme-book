# WORD: Outcome

## Meaning (plain):
The way a thing turns out; a consequence or final result of a process or event.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the "final verdict" of your hustle. Represents the inevitable result of all the work, beef, and strategy you've put in. The "outcome" is the win, the check, or the legend that you've been building toward since the beginning.

2 (out-come)

result, consequence, fallout, verdict, product, conclusion

## Antonyms:
