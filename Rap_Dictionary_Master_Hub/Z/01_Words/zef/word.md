# WORD: Zef

## Meaning (plain):
A South African counterculture movement and aesthetic (Afrikaans slang) that reclaims "common" or working-class styles with ironic, exaggerated flair.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing raw, gritty, South African street style; being unapologetically "trashy" but cool (Die Antwoord style).

## Syllables:
1 (zef)

## Part of speech:
Noun/Adjective

## Synonyms:
working-class, gritty, counterculture, common

## Antonyms:
posh, elite, refined

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: deaf, chef, ref / SLANT: death, breath / MULTI: Zef to the core, more and more, open the door
