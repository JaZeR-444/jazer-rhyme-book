# WORD: Zamarro

## Meaning (plain):
A sheepskin coat; also colloquial Spanish for a coarse, rustic man, or someone who is astute and cunning.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Being a "wolf in sheep's clothing," a cunning street operator, or a rugged leader.

## Syllables:
3 (za-mar-ro)

## Part of speech:
Noun/Adjective

## Synonyms:
cunning, astute, rugged, shepherd, coat

## Antonyms:
naive, soft, urban

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: arrow, narrow, sparrow / SLANT: shadow, hollow / MULTI: Zamarro mind, one of a kind, leave 'em behind

## 3 bar-ready examples:

2. Rugged like the coat, cunning like a Zamarro.
3. Out in the wild, I move like a Zamarro.
