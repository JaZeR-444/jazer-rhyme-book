# WORD: Batonist

## Meaning (plain):
A person who uses or specializes in the use of a baton, such as an orchestral conductor or a majorette.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for conducting the scene or being in total control of the rhythm. Represents someone who directs the energy of the crowd or the orchestra of life with a "baton" (authority or mic).

## Syllables:
3 (ba-ton-ist)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
conductor, leader, director, majorette

## Antonyms:
follower, participant, audience

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: list, mist, twist / SLANT: botanist, honest, chronic / MULTI: batonist flow, watch the music go, leader of the show

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Conducting the chaos, I'm the batonist in front.
2. Leading the parade, batonist with the stunt.
3. Every beat is directed, batonist on the hunt.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Directing/conducting the energy
2. Leadership/authority over a group
3. Orchestral/performance precision
