# WORD: Neighborhood

## Meaning (plain):
A geographically localized community within a larger area; the area or region around a place; the people living nearby.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The ultimate term for "home turf." Represents the specific blocks and social units that raised you and provided your initial street credibility. Being a product of your "neighborhood" means you carry the values and the energy of that specific local culture wherever you go.

3 (neigh-bor-hood)

noun

hood, block, vicinity, community, territory, locality

## Antonyms:
