# WORD: Wherves

## Meaning (plain):
1. Plural of wharf; structures alongside water for mooring vessels and loading cargo. 2. Alternative form of "wharve," meaning the whorl of a spindle.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the "landing spots" of the game where the heavy talent and the "goods" are delivered. Represents the high-traffic zones of the culture where the business is done. Also refers to the "spinning" or circular momentum of a career that keeps pulling everything into its whorl.
