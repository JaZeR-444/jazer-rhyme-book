# WORD: Zogo

## Meaning (plain):
In Liberia, wayward or homeless youths often involved in petty crime; or an oracle/priest among Torres Strait Islanders.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing the "street survivor" or the "spiritual guide"; moving from the bottom to the priest-level.
