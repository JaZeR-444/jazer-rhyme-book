# WORD: Girondin

## Meaning (plain):
A member of the moderate republican party during the French Revolution, representing the interests of the bourgeoisie and advocating for a decentralized government.

Metaphor for a strategic, "middle-ground" force that still carries massive influence. Represents someone who isn't an extremist but holds the keys to power and advocates for the interests of the "established" but revolutionary.
