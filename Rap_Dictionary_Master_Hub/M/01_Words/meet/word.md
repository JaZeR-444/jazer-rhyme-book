# WORD: Meet

## Meaning (plain):
To come into the presence of someone by chance or arrangement; to encounter or come into contact with; to satisfy or fulfill a requirement.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "head-on collision" with the opposition or a major move. Represents the moment when your flow "meets" the listener's expectations or when you "meet" a rival in the arena of the battle. A moment of contact and fulfillment.
