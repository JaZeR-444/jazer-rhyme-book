# WORD: Balanism

## Meaning (plain):
1. The veneration or worship of the balanus (from *balanus*, meaning acorn or barnacle). 2. (Medicine) The application or use of a suppository or pessary.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being a "barnacle" (extremely persistent and hard to remove) or for being a "remedy" that is applied directly to the problem. Represents a stubborn, immovable presence.
