# WORD: Amature

## Meaning (plain):
A common misspelling of "amateur," referring to a person who engages in a pursuit, especially a sport or art, on an unpaid rather than a professional basis.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being "raw" or "unpolished" in a way that is either pure or unprofessional. Often used as an insult to describe someone who isn't ready for the "pro" leagues.

## Syllables:
3 (am-a-teur)

## Part of speech:
noun / adjective

## Synonyms:
amateur, novice, rookie, beginner, non-professional

## Antonyms:
professional, expert, veteran, master
