# WORD: Lurries

## Meaning (plain):
1. Plural of lurry; an obsolete term for a confused heap, throng, or jumble (as of people or sounds). 2. (Obsolete/Dialectal) A variant of "lorry," meaning a large vehicle for carrying heavy goods.
