# WORD: Gabled

## Meaning (plain):
Constructed with a gable or gables (the triangular part of a wall that supports a pitched roof).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for having a high, structured, and triangular "peak." Represents a life or a career that is built with architectural precision and has a distinct, high-reaching silhouette.

## Syllables:
2 (ga-bled)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
pitched, peaked, structured, architectural

## Antonyms:
flat, low, unstructured

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: labeled, enabled, tabled / SLANT: stable, cable, label / MULTI: gabled roof, absolute proof, stay aloof

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Built the mansion gabled, reaching for the sky.
2. Architecture solid, gabled and high.
3. Every move is structured, gabled by design.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Reaching the "peak" of success
2. Architectural strength/precision
3. High-reaching silhouette or reputation

## Tags:
architectural, unique, strength, status
