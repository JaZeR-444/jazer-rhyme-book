# WORD: Baraka

## Meaning (plain):
In Islam, a blessing or spiritual power regarded as a divine gift, inhering in saints, charismatic leaders, and natural objects. It signifies an increase or benefit beyond material calculation.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The ultimate metaphor for "the touch" or having a divine blessing on your career. It represents that intangible charisma and favor that makes everything you do successful beyond just hard work.

## Syllables:
3 (ba-ra-ka)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
blessing, favor, charisma, divine-gift, grace

## Antonyms:
curse, bad-luck, misfortune
