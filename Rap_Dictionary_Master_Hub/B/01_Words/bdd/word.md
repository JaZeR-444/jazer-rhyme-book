# WORD: Bdd

## Meaning (plain):
1. (Psychology) Body Dysmorphic Disorder; a mental health condition characterized by an obsessive preoccupation with a perceived flaw in physical appearance. 2. (Tech) Behavior-Driven Development; a software development process that prioritizes communication and behavioral specs.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being "obsessed with the look" or for focusing on the "behavior" and the flow of the system. Represents a deep, sometimes painful focus on the fine details of one's persona or the way things move.
