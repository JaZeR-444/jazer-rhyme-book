# WORD: Zealot

## Meaning (plain):
A person who is fanatically devoted to a particular cause or belief, especially a religious or political one.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Someone who is "too deep" in the game, a fanatic for the craft, or a warrior for the cause.

## Syllables:
2 (zeal-ot)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
fanatic, radical, enthusiast, activist, partisan

## Antonyms:
moderate, skeptic, indifferent

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: lot, hot, pot, plot / SLANT: ballot, mallet / MULTI: Zealot for the bars, reach for the stars, leave no scars

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm a hip-hop zealot, living for the sound.
2. Fanatical devotion, zealot in the town.
3. They call me a zealot, I'm too deep to turn around.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Fanatic" level of skill/devotion
2. Radical commitment to the "Cause" (the music/community)
3. "Uncompromising" nature

## Tags:
personality, passion, spiritual, belief
