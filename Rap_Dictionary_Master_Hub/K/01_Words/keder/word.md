# WORD: Keder

## Meaning (plain):
1. A component used to create a continuous, waterproof connection between fabric and a frame (as in tents or banners). 2. (Turkish) Meaning "angst" or "sorrow." 3. A given name.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being the "continuous link" that holds everything together. Represents an unbreakable, seamless connection between the "fabric" (the art) and the "frame" ( the structure/reality). Also represents a deep, purposeful angst.

## Syllables:
2 (ke-der)

## Part of speech:
noun / adjective

## Synonyms:
link, binder, connection, angst, sorrow

## Antonyms:
separation, loose-fit, gap

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: leader, breeder, reader / SLANT: weather, better, sweater / MULTI: keder link, never blink, stay in sync

2. Unbreakable bond, I'm the industry's keder.
3. Feeling the keder, the angst of a leader.
