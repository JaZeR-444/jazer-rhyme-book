# WORD: Zattare

## Meaning (plain):
Italian plural of "zattera," meaning rafts; simple flat structures used for flotation or transport on water.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a fleet of flotation devices, staying above the water (the struggle), or a simple but effective group move.

## Syllables:
3 (zat-ta-re)

## Part of speech:
Noun (Plural)

## Synonyms:
rafts, floats, vessels

## Antonyms:
None

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: battery, flattery / SLANT: water, daughter / MULTI: Zattare on the waves, nobody saves, avoid the graves

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Staying afloat on the Zattare.
2. Fleet of rafts, we moving the Zattare.
3. Above the surface, trusting the Zattare.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Staying afloat/Survival on the water
2. "Fleet" of simple but effective tools
3. Flotation vs Sinking (success vs failure)
