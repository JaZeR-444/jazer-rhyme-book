# WORD: Karwar

## Meaning (plain):
A coastal city in Karnataka, India, known for its scenic beauty and historic significance as a port town. Often called the "Kashmir of Karnataka."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "hidden gem" or a coastal stronghold. Represents a place of beauty and value that is also a strategic entry point (a port). Carrying the energy of a scenic but powerful territory.
