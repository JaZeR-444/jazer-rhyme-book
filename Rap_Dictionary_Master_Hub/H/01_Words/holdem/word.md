# WORD: Holdem

## Meaning (plain):
A popular variant of poker (Texas Hold'em) where players receive two private cards and use five community cards to make the best hand.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a high-stakes, "all-in" move or for playing your cards close to your chest. Represents someone who knows when to bluff, when to bet big, and how to dominate the whole table.
