# WORD: Glump

## Meaning (plain):
(Colloquial) To be sullen or to sulk; a mood that is a combination of "gloomy" and "grumpy."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a heavy, low-frequency mood that carries its own kind of gravity. Represents a sullen intensity that refuses to smile or "play nice" with the mainstream.

## Syllables:
1 (glump)

## Part of speech:
verb / noun

## Synonyms:
sulk, mope, gloom, pout, heavy-mood

## Antonyms:
cheer, smile, rejoice

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: dump, jump, slump / SLANT: stump, pump, lump / MULTI: glump state, heavy weight, decide the fate
