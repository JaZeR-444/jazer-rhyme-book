# WORD: Zeal

## Meaning (plain):
Great energy or enthusiasm in pursuit of a cause or an objective.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The hunger, the drive, the "unstoppable fire" to reach the top.

## Syllables:
1 (zeal)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
passion, fervor, enthusiasm, drive, hunger

## Antonyms:
apathy, indifference, laziness

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: real, feel, steel, deal / SLANT: still, thrill / MULTI: Zeal for the game, never gonna be the same, chase the fame

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I move with zeal, everything I say is real.
2. Hunger in the eyes, you can feel the zeal.
3. Unstoppable drive, fueled by the zeal.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Unstoppable hunger" for success
2. Passion vs Apathy
3. "Real" vs "Zeal" (authenticity and drive)
