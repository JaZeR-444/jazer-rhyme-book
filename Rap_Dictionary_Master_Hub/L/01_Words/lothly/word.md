# WORD: Lothly

## Meaning (plain):
1. In a reluctant or unwilling manner. 2. In a loathsome, hateful, or disgusting way.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being a "hateful" presence or for moving with a reluctant, almost forced intensity. Represents someone who is repulsive to the mainstream but carries a heavy, dark energy.

## Syllables:
2 (loth-ly)

## Part of speech:
adverb / adjective

## Synonyms:
reluctantly, loathsomely, hatefully, unwillingly

## Antonyms:
eagerly, pleasantly, willingly

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: sky, high, fly / SLANT: only, lonely, slowly / MULTI: lothly state, filled with hate, never late

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Moving lothly through the city, no love in the heart.
2. Reluctant energy, lothly from the start.
3. Hateful with the rhythm, lothly in the art.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Reluctant/forced intensity
2. "Hateful" or repulsive presence
3. Lack of eagerness/compliance

## Tags:
archaic, unique, attitude, emotion
