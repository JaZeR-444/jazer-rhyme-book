# WORD: Gee

## Meaning (plain):
1. (Interjection) Expressing surprise or emphasis. 2. (Command) A direction to a horse to turn right or move ahead. 3. (Noun) A unit of force equal to gravity (g-force).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for intense "G-force" pressure or for being the one who gives the "gee" (go) command to the team. Represents a heavy, gravitational pull or an authoritative signal to advance.
