# WORD: Beknots

## Meaning (plain):
To tie a knot or knots in something; to entangle or make complicated.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a flow that is incredibly complex and "tangled" in a way that others can't unravel. Represents a high level of lyrical entanglement that leaves the competition confused.

## Syllables:
2 (be-knots)

## Part of speech:
verb

## Synonyms:
entangle, knot, complicate, weave, tangle

## Antonyms:
unravel, untie, simplify, clarify

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: thoughts, slots, plots / SLANT: pots, shots, knots / MULTI: beknots the brain, complex in the rain, never plain

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Flow so complex it beknots the whole mind.
2. Lyrical web, beknots the design.
3. Entangled in the rhythm, beknots in the line.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Complexity/entanglement of flow
2. Leaving the competition confused/tangled
3. Making "knots" out of simple ideas

## Tags:
action, unique, intellect, complexity
