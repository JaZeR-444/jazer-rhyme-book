# WORD: Zircon

## Meaning (plain):
A common mineral (zirconium silicate) found in various rocks; often used as a gemstone (diamond simulant) and as a source of zirconium metal.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "diamond simulant" (looking like wealth) or being a durable, natural source of "metallic" strength.

## Syllables:
2 (zir-con)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
gemstone, mineral, crystal, simulant

## Antonyms:
None
