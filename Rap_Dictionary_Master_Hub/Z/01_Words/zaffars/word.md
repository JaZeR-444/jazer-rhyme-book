# WORD: Zaffars

## Meaning (plain):
Plural of zaffar (victory) or plural of zaffar/zaffre (cobalt oxide compounds).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Multiple victories, a streak of wins, or a collection of rare, valuable "blue" pigments (jewelry/value).

## Syllables:
2 (zaf-fars)

## Part of speech:
Noun (Plural)

## Synonyms:
victories, triumphs, zaffres

## Antonyms:
defeats, losses

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: stars, cars, bars / SLANT: offers, coffers / MULTI: Zaffars in the bag, never let 'em sag, victory flag

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Collecting Zaffars like I'm hunting for the blue.
2. A streak of Zaffars, what else can I do?
3. Counting up the Zaffars, we win through and through.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Winning streak (multiple victories)
2. Collection of rare values/pigments
3. Stars vs Zaffars (rhyme)
