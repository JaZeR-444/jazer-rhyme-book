# WORD: Trio

## Meaning (plain):
A group or set of three people or things, especially three musicians or singers performing together; a musical composition for three voices.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "trifecta" of power. Represents a three-person crew or a three-part strategy that is perfectly coordinated and unstoppable. Being part of a "trio" means you have three distinct voices or forces coming together to dominate the scene.

## Syllables:
2 (tri-o)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
trifecta, threesome, triad, group-of-three, ensemble

## Antonyms:
solo, duo, mass, crowd
