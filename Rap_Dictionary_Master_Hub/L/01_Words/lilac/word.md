# WORD: Lilac

## Meaning (plain):
A widely cultivated shrub (*Syringa vulgaris*) known for its large clusters of fragrant pinkish-purple or white flowers; a pale purple color.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "fragrant" and beautiful success that comes in clusters. Represents a soft, aesthetic brilliance that carries its own distinct "color" and presence in the garden of the scene.
