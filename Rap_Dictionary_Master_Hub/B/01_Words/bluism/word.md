# WORD: Bluism

## Meaning (plain):
(Archaic/Derogatory) A term for "blueism," referring to the possession or affectation of learning or literary interests, particularly in a woman (from "blue-stocking").

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for intellectual depth or for "flexing" your knowledge over the crowd. Represents someone who carries a "blue" (intellectual or sad) scholarly energy that sets them apart from the street level.

2 (blu-ism)

noun

intellectualism, scholarship, affectation, blue-stocking

## Antonyms:
ignorance, simplicity, illiteracy
