# WORD: Haram

## Meaning (plain):
An Arabic term meaning forbidden, taboo, or prohibited by Islamic law.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a lifestyle or a flow that is "forbidden" or breaks all the conventional rules. Represents someone who moves in the taboo spaces and isn't afraid to challenge the established "laws" of the industry.
