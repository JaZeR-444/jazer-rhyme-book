# WORD: Xylometer

## Meaning (plain):
An instrument used to determine the specific gravity or density of wood.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for "measuring the weight" of the competition. Being a "xylometer" means you have the ability to see through the hype and determine the real "density" or value of a rapper's sound. A status of being the ultimate judge of street gravity.
