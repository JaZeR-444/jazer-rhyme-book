# WORD: Zoned

## Meaning (plain):
Past tense of zone; divided into areas; or informal for being in a daze or hyper-focused ("zoned out," "zoned in").

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Already locked into the flow; having secured the territory or being completely focused on the target.
