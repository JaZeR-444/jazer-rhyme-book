# WORD: Barnaby

## Meaning (plain):
A masculine given name derived from the New Testament name Barnabas, meaning "son of consolation" or "son of exhortation." Also an Old English surname meaning "young warrior settlement."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being a "young warrior" or a source of "consolation" (comfort or support) for the team. Represents a mix of combat-readiness and emotional intelligence.
