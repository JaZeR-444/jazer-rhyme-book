# WORD: Falerno

## Meaning (plain):
A famous ancient Roman wine, noted for its high quality and potency, grown in the Falernian region near Naples.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for "ancient quality" or for being a potent, high-end force in the scene. Represents a status that is timeless, intoxicating, and historically respected.
