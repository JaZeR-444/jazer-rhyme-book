# WORD: Ability

## Meaning (plain):
Possession of the means or skill to do something; talent, skill, or proficiency in a particular area.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the raw toolkit and technical prowess a rapper brings. It's not just talent; it's the proven "ability" to execute complex flows and maintain control under pressure.

## Syllables:
4 (a-bil-i-ty)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
talent, skill, proficiency, capability, competence

## Antonyms:
inability, weakness, incompetence, incapacity

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: city, gritty, pretty / SLANT: stability, mobility, agility / MULTI: natural ability, peak stability, show the agility

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Natural ability, I was born for the mic.
2. Showing the ability, every line that I write.
3. Beyond just a talent, it's a refined ability.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Raw technical prowess
2. Having the "tools" to succeed
3. Skill vs. luck contrast

## Tags:
status, intellect, common
