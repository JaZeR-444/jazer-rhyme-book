# WORD: Akawai

## Meaning (plain):
An indigenous Cariban-speaking people of the upper Mazaruni River basin in Guyana, also known as the Akawaio.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for indigenous strength, river-basin resilience, or a voice from the deep jungle. Represents a connection to ancient, uncolonized roots.
