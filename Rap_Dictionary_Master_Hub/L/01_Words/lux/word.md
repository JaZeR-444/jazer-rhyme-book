# WORD: Lux

## Meaning (plain):
The SI unit of illuminance and luminous emittance, measuring luminous flux per unit area (one lumen per square meter).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the literal amount of "light" or attention someone brings to the scene. Represents a high-level "shine" that illuminates everything around it. When you turn up the Lux, you own the spotlight.
