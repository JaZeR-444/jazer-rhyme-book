# WORD: Fade

## Meaning (plain):
To lose freshness, strength, or vitality; to lose brilliance of color; to disappear gradually.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for losing relevance or slowly being forgotten by the culture. "Catching a fade" can also mean getting defeated in a fight or disappearing from a situation. Represents the slow death of a career or a persona.

## Syllables:
1 (fade)

## Part of speech:
verb / noun

## Synonyms:
vanish, decline, wither, dim, disappear

## Antonyms:
bloom, brighten, appear, strengthen

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: made, paid, shade / SLANT: age, cage, stage / MULTI: catch a fade, getting paid, in the shade

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Watch the legends fade while I stay in the light.
2. Don't let your spirit fade, stay in the fight.
3. Caught a quick fade, now they're out of the sight.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Losing relevance/fame
2. Defeat in a fight ("catch a fade")
3. Gradual disappearance from the top

movement, status, common, attitude
