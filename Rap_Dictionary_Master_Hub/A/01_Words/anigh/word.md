# WORD: Anigh

## Meaning (plain):
(Archaic or Poetic) Near or nearly; close in place or time.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being "close to the edge" or "nearly there." Carrying a poetic, vintage weight when describing proximity to success, danger, or a goal.

## Syllables:
2 (a-nigh)

## Part of speech:
adverb / preposition

## Synonyms:
near, nearly, close, adjacent

## Antonyms:
afar, distant, far

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: sky, high, fly / SLANT: eye, cry, shy / MULTI: anigh the gate, nearly late, decide the fate

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Success is drawing anigh, I can feel the heat.
2. Walking anigh the edge, balanced on the beat.
3. Every step anigh the goal, never in retreat.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Proximity to success/the goal
2. "Edge" or danger metaphors
3. Poetic/vintage weight for "near"

## Tags:
archaic, poetic, unique
