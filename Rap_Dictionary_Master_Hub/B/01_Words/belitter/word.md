# WORD: Belitter

## Meaning (plain):
1. To strew with litter or rubbish; to make a place messy or disordered. 2. To bring forth a litter (to give birth to a group of young).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for "messing up" the scene with too much dominance or for being extremely prolific (birthing a new "litter" of tracks/ideas). Represents a messy, organic, and unstoppable output.

## Syllables:
3 (be-lit-ter)

## Part of speech:
verb

## Synonyms:
strew, clutter, birth, produce, mess-up

## Antonyms:
clean, organize, barren, sterile

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: bitter, hitter, glitter / SLANT: winter, splinter, center / MULTI: belitter the floor, hit 'em with more, open the door

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I belitter the floor with the wreckage of the weak.
2. Prolific with the flow, belitter while I speak.
3. Messy dominance, belitter with the peak.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Being "too much" for the scene (clutter)
2. Prolific creation (birthing a litter)
3. Disordering the "clean" systems of others

## Tags:
action, unique, growth, attitude
