# WORD: Zig

## Meaning (plain):
A sharp turn or change of direction in a course; to move in one of the directions followed in a zigzag pattern.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
A quick directional shift in the flow, dodging the expected, or making a sharp move to stay ahead.

## Syllables:
1 (zig)

## Part of speech:
Noun/Verb

## Synonyms:
turn, shift, dodge, swerve

## Antonyms:
zag, straight

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: big, dig, fig, jig / SLANT: stick, trick / MULTI: Zig in the lane, no one can restrain, avoid the strain

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. When they expect a zag, I hit 'em with the Zig.
2. Dodging every trap, making every move big.
3. Quick with the turn, yeah I'm hitting the Zig.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Unpredictability ("Zig while they zag")
2. Dodging obstacles/haters
3. Quick reflexes/High energy

movement, direction, speed, agility
