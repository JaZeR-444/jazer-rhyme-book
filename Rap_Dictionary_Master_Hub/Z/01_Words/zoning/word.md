# WORD: Zoning

## Meaning (plain):
The process of dividing an area into zones for specific land use; or the act of dividing into sections.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing "dividing and conquering" the territory; setting the boundaries and deciding who belongs where.

## Syllables:
2 (zo-ning)

## Part of speech:

## Synonyms:
dividing, mapping, sectioning, regulating

## Antonyms:

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: owning, phoning, moaning / SLANT: flowing, knowing / MULTI: Zoning the block, key in the lock, stop the clock
