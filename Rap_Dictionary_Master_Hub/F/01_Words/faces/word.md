# WORD: Faces

## Meaning (plain):
1. Plural of face; the front parts of heads. 2. Planar surfaces of 3D geometric figures. 3. Various aspects or characters of someone.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the multifaceted personas a rapper must maintain. Represents the "mask" you wear for the crowd vs. the one you show to the streets. Also refers to the "faces" (surface area) of your influence.
