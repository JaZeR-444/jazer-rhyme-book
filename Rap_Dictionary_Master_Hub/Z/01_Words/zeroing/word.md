# WORD: Zeroing

## Meaning (plain):
The process of adjusting a device or instrument to zero; adjusting firearm sights; narrowing focus onto a specific target.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing the "countdown" to the strike, narrowing the focus on the prize, or recalibrating the strategy.

## Syllables:
3 (ze-ro-ing)

## Part of speech:
Verb (Present Participle)

## Synonyms:
aligning, focusing, targeting, recalibrating

## Antonyms:
distracting, scattering

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: wing, ring, sing, king / SLANT: flowing, knowing / MULTI: Zeroing the aim, never be the same, master of the game
