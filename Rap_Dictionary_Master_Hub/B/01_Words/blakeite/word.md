# WORD: Blakeite

## Meaning (plain):
A rare mineral composed of an iron tellurite, originally found in the mines of Goldfield, Nevada.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being a "rare find" or a unique, valuable element in the scene. Represents something that is hidden deep in the "mines" (the underground) but is incredibly solid and distinct.
