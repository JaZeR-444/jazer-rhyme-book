# WORD: Aurora

## Meaning (plain):
A natural electrical phenomenon characterized by the appearance of streamers of reddish or greenish light in the sky.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Colorful, rare, cosmic lights.

## Syllables:
3 (au-ro-ra)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
Dawn, light, luminescence

## Antonyms:
Darkness

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):

SLANT: horror, scorer
MULTI: a roar a, saw her
