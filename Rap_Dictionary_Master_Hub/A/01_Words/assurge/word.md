# WORD: Assurge

## Meaning (plain):
To rise up; to soar or ascend (from Latin *assurgere*).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for an explosive rise to the top or for a flow that "soars" above the average. Represents the act of ascending with power and elegance.

## Syllables:
2 (as-surge)

## Part of speech:
verb

## Synonyms:
ascend, soar, rise, mount, elevate

## Antonyms:
descend, fall, decline, sink

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: urge, merge, purge / SLANT: verge, dirge, splurge / MULTI: assurge high, touch the sky, watch 'em sigh

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Watch the career assurge, I'm reaching for the peak.
2. Flow so high it'll assurge, leave the opposition weak.
3. Ascending with the rhythm, assurge while I speak.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Powerful ascent/rising up
2. Soaring above the competition
3. Elegance in elevation

## Tags:
latin, movement, unique, power
