# WORD: Zapper

## Meaning (plain):
An electronic device used to attract and kill insects (bug zapper); also an informal term for a television remote control.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing someone who "zaps" the pests (haters), controls the "channels" (the narrative), or has an electric, shocking impact.

## Syllables:
2 (zap-per)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
bug zapper, remote control, clicker, shocker

## Antonyms:
None

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: trapper, rapper, dapper, snapper / SLANT: chapter, factor / MULTI: Zapper in hand, control the land, best in the band

2. Attract the bugs then zap 'em, a real Zapper.
3. Shocking the system, they call me the Zapper.
