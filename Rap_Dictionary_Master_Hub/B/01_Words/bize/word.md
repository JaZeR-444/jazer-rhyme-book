# WORD: Bize

## Meaning (plain):
A dry, cold wind from the north or northeast in southeastern France and Switzerland. (Also spelled *bise*).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a chilling, cold-hearted intensity that blows through the scene. Represents a "breath of fresh air" that is sharp, cold, and clears away the weak "warmth" of the mainstream.
