# WORD: Albatrossing

## Meaning (plain):
Flying long distances without flapping wings (gliding). Also a golf reference (scoring -2 on a single hole), symbolizing a rare, high-skill victory.

## Sonic Texture:
Wide, Majestic.

## Contrast:
vs. Flapping / Low-skill.

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Albatrossing in the sky.
