# WORD: Achete

## Meaning (plain):
Derived from the French verb "acheter," meaning to buy or purchase. In older English or specific legal contexts, it may refer to an escheat or purchase.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Used to describe high-end acquisitions or the act of purchasing status, often with a sophisticated or European flair.
