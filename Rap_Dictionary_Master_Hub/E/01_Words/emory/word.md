# WORD: Emory

## Meaning (plain):
An English given name and surname, a variant of Emery, derived from Old German meaning "home strength" or "industrious leader."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for "home strength" and being an industrious leader in your own territory. Represents someone who is grounded in their roots and has the work ethic to lead the whole "house" (crew or family).
