# WORD: Lawyer

## Meaning (plain):
A person qualified to offer legal advice, draft legal documents, or represent individuals in legal matters.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for someone who is a master of language, negotiation, and the "fine print" of the game. Represents the person who defends the crew's reputation or handles the complex contractual side of the hustle. A verbal "defense" specialist.

## Syllables:
2 (law-yer)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
attorney, counsel, advocate, solicitor, mouthpiece

## Antonyms:
criminal, client, prosecutor

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: fire, higher, choir / SLANT: foyer, employer, destroyer / MULTI: call the lawyer, job of a destroyer, high-end employer

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I don't talk to the ops, I talk through the lawyer.
2. Master of the language, verbal lawyer.
3. Every move is legal, lawyer in the foyer.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Mastery of "fine print"/language
2. Defending the reputation/status
3. Professional/judicial weight
