# WORD: Zeks

## Meaning (plain):
Russian slang for prisoners or inmates, specifically those in the Soviet Union's gulag system.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing survival in the "system," being a veteran of the struggle, or having a hardened, prisoner-of-war mindset.

1 (zeks)

## Part of speech:
