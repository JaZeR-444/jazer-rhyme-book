# WORD: Boshvark

## Meaning (plain):
A Southern African name for the bushpig (*Potamochoerus larvatus*), known for being a tough, nocturnal, and sometimes aggressive animal.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being a "dirty" but tough fighter who thrives in the "bush" (the street or untamed environment). Represents a nocturnal, aggressive, and unyielding energy that doesn't mind getting messy to win.
