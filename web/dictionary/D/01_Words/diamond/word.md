# WORD: Diamond

## Meaning (plain):
native crystalline carbon that is the hardest known mineral, that is usually nearly colorless, that when transparent and free from flaws is highly valued as a precious stone, and that is used industrially especially as an abrasive; also : a piece of this substance; crystallized carbon produced artificially; something that resembles a diamond (as in brilliance, value, or fine quality)
