# WORD: Zakuska

## Meaning (plain):
A Russian term for hors d'oeuvres or snacks, typically served with vodka; literally means "little bites" or "chaser."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The "starter" before the main course, small wins, or a "chaser" for a hard shot (life/struggle).

## Syllables:
3 (za-kus-ka)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
appetizer, snack, chaser, hors d'oeuvre

## Antonyms:
None

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: dusk, musk, rusk / SLANT: trust ya, must ya / MULTI: Zakuska plate, before the main state, don't be late

## 3 bar-ready examples:

2. Little bites of victory, Zakuska of the force.
3. Chasing the shot with a cold Zakuska.
