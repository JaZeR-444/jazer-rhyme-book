# WORD: Zygomorphous

## Meaning (plain):
Describing a flower that is bilaterally symmetrical (can be divided into two equal halves along only one plane).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Having a "perfectly balanced" but "specific" style; a flow that is symmetrical and harmonious only from a certain perspective.

## Syllables:
4 (zy-go-mor-phous)

## Part of speech:
Adjective

## Synonyms:
bilateral, symmetrical, balanced, specific

## Antonyms:
actinomorphous (radially symmetrical)

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: thus, bus, fuss, plus / SLANT: focus, lotus / MULTI: Zygomorphous bloom, in every room, clear the gloom

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Balanced and symmetrical, purely Zygomorphous.
2. Only one way to see it, flow is Zygomorphous.
3. Specific beauty, spirit Zygomorphous.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Bilateral Symmetry" (Balance)
2. "Specific Perspective" (Unique flow)
3. Biological/Floral beauty
