# WORD: Glitch

## Meaning (plain):
A small, temporary technical fault or problem that prevents something from working correctly.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for disrupting the system or for having a "supernatural" edge that looks like a technical error to the competition. Represents a move that is so fast or unique it "breaks" the rules of the game.

## Syllables:
1 (glitch)

## Part of speech:
noun / verb

## Synonyms:
error, bug, fault, disruption, hitch

## Antonyms:
smooth-operation, flow, perfection

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: rich, pitch, switch / SLANT: hitch, stitch, twitch / MULTI: glitch in the code, high gold, never fold

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm a glitch in the matrix, they can't track the move.
2. System error, glitch in the groove.
3. Disrupting the scene, glitch with the proof.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Disrupting the established system
2. "Unreal" or supernatural performance
3. Technical/digital metaphors

## Tags:
tech, common, unique, movement
