# WORD: Game

## Meaning (plain):
1. An activity engaged in for amusement or pleasure. 2. (Slang) Knowledge, skill, or prowess in a particular activity, especially in street life or dating.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The entire arena of rap and street life. Having "game" means having the knowledge, skill, and presence to navigate the industry and the streets successfully. It represents the rules, the players, and the ultimate competition.
