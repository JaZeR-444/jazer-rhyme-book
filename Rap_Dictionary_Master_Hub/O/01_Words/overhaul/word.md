# WORD: Overhaul

## Meaning (plain):
To make necessary repairs on and restore to serviceable condition; to investigate and examine thoroughly for repair or revision; to completely renovate.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "total career reset" or rebuilding your sound from the ground up. Represents the process of stripping everything away and renovating your flow until it's in high-end, serviceable condition again. A complete transformation of the hustle.

## Syllables:
3 (o-ver-haul)

## Part of speech:
verb / noun

## Synonyms:
renovate, rebuild, repair, restore, investigate, fix

## Antonyms:
neglect, destroy, abandon, break

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: ball, call, wall / SLANT: control, soul, whole / MULTI: complete overhaul, purely call, through the wall

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I had to give my flow an overhaul, start from the roots.
2. Renovating the rhythm, overhaul in the boots.
3. Every move is a repair, overhaul for the shoots.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Total career/sound rebuilding
2. Reaching "serviceable" condition again
3. Stripping down and starting over

## Tags:
action, common, unique, transformation
