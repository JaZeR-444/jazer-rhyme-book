# WORD: Ascula

## Meaning (plain):
A stage in the early development of certain sponges, occurring after the free-swimming larva attaches itself to a surface.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the stage of "attachment" or finally finding a solid foundation to build on. Represents the transition from a wandering "larva" to a permanent, growing force.
