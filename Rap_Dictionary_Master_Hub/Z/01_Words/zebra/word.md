# WORD: Zebra

## Meaning (plain):
An African wild horse with black-and-white stripes and an erect mane.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "black and white" (clear/direct) perspective, standing out in the crowd with a unique pattern, or "stripes" earned in the streets.

## Syllables:
2 (ze-bra)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
equine, striped horse

## Antonyms:
None

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
