# WORD: Zygadenus

## Meaning (plain):
A genus of flowering plants (death camas) that are fatally toxic to grazing animals; found in North America.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "deadly flower"; blooming in the field but lethal to anyone who tries to "graze" (take a piece) of the success.
