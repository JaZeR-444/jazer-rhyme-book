# WORD: Mechanism

## Meaning (plain):
A system or structure of moving parts that performs a function; the agency or means by which an effect is produced or a purpose is accomplished.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the "underlying system" that makes your career work. Represents the tactical moves and the structural "machinery" (team, strategy, connections) that produces your success. The hidden engine behind the public victory.

## Syllables:
3 (mech-a-nism)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
system, agency, means, device, process, structure

## Antonyms:
disarray, randomness, failure

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: schism, prism, rhythm / SLANT: wisdom, vision, criticism / MULTI: mechanism flow, let the system grow, watch 'em go

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Every move is part of the mechanism, purely precise.
2. Underneath the success is a heavy mechanism.
3. Agency of the win, mechanism within.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Underlying tactical system
2. Structural/strategic "machinery"
3. Producing effects through hidden means
