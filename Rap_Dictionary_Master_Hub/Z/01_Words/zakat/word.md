# WORD: Zakat

## Meaning (plain):
An obligatory charitable donation in Islam, typically 2.5% of one's wealth, intended for the poor and needy.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The "tax" for the community, staying righteous with the riches, or sharing the pie with the brothers.

## Syllables:
2 (za-kat)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
zakah, almsgiving, charity, tax

## Antonyms:
greed, selfishness
