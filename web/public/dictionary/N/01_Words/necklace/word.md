# WORD: Necklace

## Meaning (plain):
A piece of jewelry worn around the neck, typically consisting of a chain or string of decorative elements like jewels, beads, or gems.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The ultimate status symbol. Represents the "chain" of success and the literal and metaphorical "weight" around your neck that signals wealth and dominance. Having a heavy necklace means you've made it to the top tiers of the game.
