# WORD: Mazzard

## Meaning (plain):
A wild sweet cherry tree (Prunus avium), often used as a rootstock for grafting; the fruit of this tree.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "raw" and natural excellence that serves as the foundation for others. Represents the original "wild" energy that people "graft" onto to try and be successful. You are the rootstock of the flavor.
