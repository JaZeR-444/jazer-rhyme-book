# WORD: Glacious

## Meaning (plain):
Of the nature of or characteristic of ice; icy; frozen.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The ultimate metaphor for "cold-hearted" dominance. Represents a flow or persona that is completely frozen, slick, and unforgiving. Turning the room "glacious" means dropping the temperature with pure intensity.

## Syllables:
2 (gla-cious)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
icy, frozen, gelid, cold, slick

## Antonyms:
warm, fiery, hot, molten

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: precious, spacious, gracious / SLANT: anxious, cautious, ambitious / MULTI: glacious flow, watch the ice grow, cold as the snow

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Everything I spit is glacious, feel the chill.
2. Cold-hearted monster, glacious with the will.
3. Frozen in the moment, glacious while I kill.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Cold-hearted" or icy nature
2. "Slick" like ice (unforgiving)
3. Lowering the temperature of the scene

## Tags:
nature, unique, intensity, attitude
