# WORD: Zambia

## Meaning (plain):
A landlocked country in Southern Africa, formerly Northern Rhodesia; known for its natural resources (copper) and the Victoria Falls.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing independence, natural wealth (copper/resources), or the "thunder" of the smoke (Victoria Falls).

## Syllables:
3 (zam-bi-a)

## Part of speech:
Noun (Proper Name)

## Synonyms:
Republic of Zambia, Northern Rhodesia (former)

## Antonyms:
None
