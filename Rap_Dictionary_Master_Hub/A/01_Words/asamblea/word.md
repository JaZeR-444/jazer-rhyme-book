# WORD: Asamblea

## Meaning (plain):
A Spanish word meaning "assembly," "meeting," or "gathering." Refers to a group of people gathered for a common purpose or a deliberative body.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a gathering of the crew or a formal summit where the "laws" of the game are discussed. Represents collective power and unity.

## Syllables:
4 (a-sam-ble-a)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
assembly, meeting, gathering, summit, council

## Antonyms:
dispersal, separation, solitude

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: day, lay, stay / SLANT: familia, area, idea / MULTI: asamblea call, never fall, standing tall

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Calling the asamblea, the whole crew gathered.
2. Decisions in the asamblea, everything that mattered.
3. Unified in asamblea, nothing remains scattered.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Collective strength/unity
2. Formal "summit" or decision-making
3. Multilingual flair for a gathering
