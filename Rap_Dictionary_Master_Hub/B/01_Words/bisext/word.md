# WORD: Bisect

## Meaning (plain):
To divide or cut something into two equal parts.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for cutting through the noise or splitting the competition right down the middle. Represents a clean, surgical strike that divides the "real" from the "fake."

## Syllables:
2 (bi-sect)

## Part of speech:
verb

## Synonyms:
halve, divide, split, cut, separate

## Antonyms:
join, unite, combine, merge

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: project, collect, correct / SLANT: direct, expect, respect / MULTI: bisect the track, cut the pack, never look back

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm here to bisect the crowd, real on the left.
2. Surgical with the bars, bisect the theft.
3. Split the scene in two, bisect what's new.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Dividing the "real" from the "fake"
2. Surgical/precise cutting of a rival
3. Splitting the energy of a room

## Tags:
math, action, common, precision
