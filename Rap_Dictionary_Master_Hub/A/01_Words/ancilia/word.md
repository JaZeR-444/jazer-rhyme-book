# WORD: Ancilia

## Meaning (plain):
The plural of ancile; the twelve sacred shields of the Romans, one of which was said to have fallen from heaven as a pledge of the empire's safety.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for divine protection or having a set of "shields" (loyal crew, status, or talent) that guarantee safety and success. Represents celestial protection for the "empire."
