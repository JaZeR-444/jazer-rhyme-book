# WORD: Mammee

## Meaning (plain):
A tall tropical American tree (Mammea americana) with edible fruit (mammee apple) featuring a thick, leathery rind and juicy yellow flesh.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "tough-skinned" but sweet success. Represents someone who has built a tall, enduring career with a leathery "rind" that protects the high-quality, valuable "flesh" of their art. An exotic, high-altitude status.
