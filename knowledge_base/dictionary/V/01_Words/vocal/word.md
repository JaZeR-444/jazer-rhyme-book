# WORD: Vocal

## Meaning (plain):
Of or relating to the human voice; expressing opinions freely or loudly; outspoken.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for having a "loud and undeniable" delivery. Being "vocal" means you're not afraid to speak your mind and your voice is the most powerful instrument in the room. Represents someone who commands the conversation and whose message is clear, high-frequency, and unavoidable.

2 (vo-cal)

## Part of speech:
adjective / noun

outspoken, voiced, loud, articulate, expressive, clear

## Antonyms:
