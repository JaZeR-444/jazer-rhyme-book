# WORD: Antepast

## Meaning (plain):
An archaic term for a foretaste; specifically, a first course or appetizer intended to stimulate the appetite.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "sample" or a preview of what's to come. The "intro" or the first bars that set the stage for the full impact of the album or verse.

## Antonyms:
