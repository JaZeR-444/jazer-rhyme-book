# WORD: Zanjona

## Meaning (plain):
A variant of "zanja," meaning an irrigation canal, ditch, or trench dug into the ground.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing the "trenches" (the grind), a path for the "water" (money/flow) to travel, or a deep-rooted structure in the soil.

## Syllables:
3 (zan-jo-na)

## Part of speech:
