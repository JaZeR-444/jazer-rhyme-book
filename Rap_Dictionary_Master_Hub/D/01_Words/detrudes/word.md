# WORD: Detrudes

## Meaning (plain):
To thrust or force down, out, or away; to push something forcefully in a downward or outward direction.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for forcefully removing an opponent or "pushing down" any opposition. Represents a high-intensity move where you clear the path by force.
