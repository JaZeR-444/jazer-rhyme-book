# WORD: Medicine

## Meaning (plain):
The science and practice of caring for patients and promoting health; any drug or remedy for use in treating or preventing disease.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The ultimate metaphor for "the truth." Represents bars that might be bitter or hard to swallow but are exactly what the listener needs to hear. To "take your medicine" means to accept the defeat or the harsh reality delivered by a superior rapper.
