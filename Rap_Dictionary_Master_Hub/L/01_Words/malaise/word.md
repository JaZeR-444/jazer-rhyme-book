# WORD: Malaise

## Meaning (plain):
An indefinite feeling of debility or lack of health, often indicating the onset of an illness; a vague sense of mental or moral ill-being.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "vague discomfort" or a sense that something is wrong with the scene. Represents a low-energy, uneasy state where the competition feels weak or the culture has lost its "moral" compass. Carrying a heavy, sickly energy.

discomfort, debility, ill-being, uneasiness, sickness

## Antonyms:
