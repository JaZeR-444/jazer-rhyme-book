# WORD: Abetment

## Meaning (plain):
to actively second and encourage (something, such as an activity or plan); to assist or support (someone) in the achievement of a purpose; especially : to assist, encourage, instigate, or support with criminal intent in attempting or carrying out a crime —often used in the phrase aid and abet
