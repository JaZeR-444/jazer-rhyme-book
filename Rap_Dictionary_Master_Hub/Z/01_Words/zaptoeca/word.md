# WORD: Zaptoeca

## Meaning (plain):
Most likely a variant or misspelling of Zapoteca (Zapotec), referring to the indigenous people of southern Mexico (Oaxaca) known as "People of the Clouds."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing the "People of the Clouds," high-altitude perspective, and deep Mexican indigenous roots.
