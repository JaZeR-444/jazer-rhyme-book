# WORD: Beteach

## Meaning (plain):
(Archaic) 1. To hand over, deliver up, or yield. 2. To entrust or commit to the care of another. 3. To teach or instruct.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for delivering a "lesson" to the crowd or for yielding your truth to the microphone. Represents the act of committing your energy and your message to the culture.

## Syllables:
2 (be-teach)

## Part of speech:
verb

deliver, yield, instruct, entrust, commit

## Antonyms:
withhold, keep, learn, follow
