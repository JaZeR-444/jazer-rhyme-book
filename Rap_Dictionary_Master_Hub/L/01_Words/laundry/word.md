# WORD: Laundry

## Meaning (plain):
The washing of clothing and textiles; clothes that are to be washed or have been washed; a place where washing is done.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for "cleaning up" a messy situation or for handling the dirty business of the streets. Represents the process of taking the raw, "soiled" elements of life and refining them until they are fresh, ironed, and ready for display.

## Syllables:
2 (laun-dry)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
washing, cleaning, textiles, fresh-start, linen

## Antonyms:
dirt, filth, mess, soil

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: dry, sky, fly / SLANT: boundary, foundry, laundry / MULTI: laundry day, clean the way, stay in play
