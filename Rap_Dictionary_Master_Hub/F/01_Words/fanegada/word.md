# WORD: Fanegada

## Meaning (plain):
A traditional unit of land measure in Spanish-speaking countries, representing the amount of land needed to sow a fanega of grain (roughly 1.25 to 1.75 acres).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for "claiming your space" or measuring the scale of your harvest. Represents a concrete amount of territory owned or dominated in the scene.
