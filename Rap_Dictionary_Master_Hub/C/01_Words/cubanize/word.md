# WORD: Cubanize

## Meaning (plain):
To make something Cuban in quality, interests, or character; to bring under Cuban influence or style.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for infusing a track or a vibe with high-level intensity, rhythmic flavor, or "island" heat. Represents transforming a standard sound into something bold, flavorful, and culturally rich.

3 (Cu-ban-ize)

## Part of speech:
verb
