# WORD: Asimen

## Meaning (plain):
1. (Sanskrit) Unlimited or limitless. 2. (Indian) One who is guided or directed by God. 3. (African Folklore) A living vampiric witch in Dahomey tradition.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for limitless potential or being a "force of nature" that is divinely guided. Can also represent a predatory, "vampiric" hunger for success or for draining the energy of the competition.

## Syllables:
3 (a-si-men)

## Part of speech:
noun / adjective

## Synonyms:
limitless, infinite, guided, predatory, vampire

## Antonyms:
limited, finite, earthly

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: men, pen, glen / SLANT: risen, listen, mission / MULTI: asimen force, limitless course, divinely source

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Potential is asimen, no boundaries in sight.
2. Predatory flow, asimen in the night.
3. Guided by the higher power, asimen with the light.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Limitless scale/potential
2. Divine guidance/favor
3. Predatory/vampiric energy
