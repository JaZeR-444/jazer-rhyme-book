# WORD: Beatles

## Meaning (plain):
The legendary English rock band from Liverpool, formed in 1960, widely regarded as the most influential and successful act in the history of popular music.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for legendary, universal fame and being the "gold standard" for group success. Represents reaching a level of influence where you change the entire culture.
