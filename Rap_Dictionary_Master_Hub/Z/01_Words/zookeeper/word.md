# WORD: Zookeeper

## Meaning (plain):
A person who maintains or cares for animals in a zoo, responsible for their feeding, health, and daily care.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing the "handler" of the beasts; the one who controls the wild energy and keeps the animals (the squad) fed and focused.

## Syllables:
3 (zoo-keep-er)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
handler, warden, feeder, controller, curator

## Antonyms:
animal, predator (wild)

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: sleeper, deeper, creeper, reaper / SLANT: feature, teacher / MULTI: Zookeeper key, set the beast free, walk rapidly

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Controlling the chaos, call me the Zookeeper.
2. I feed the lions, I'm the main Zookeeper.
3. Locked in the cage, wait for the Zookeeper.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Handling the Beasts" (Leadership/Control)
2. "Feeding the Squad" (Financial/Moral support)
3. "Zookeeper/Sleeper" (Waking up the beasts rhyme)
