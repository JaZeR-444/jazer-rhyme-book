# WORD: Duftry

## Meaning (plain):
(India) A servant in an office whose duties include dusting and binding records, ruling paper, and making envelopes; an office boy or clerical assistant.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being the "binder" or the one who organizes the "records" (history or tracks) of the scene. Represents someone who knows the foundations and keeps the "envelopes" (money or messages) moving.
