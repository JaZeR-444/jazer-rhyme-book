# WORD: Coteau

## Meaning (plain):
A French term for a hillside, slope, or small hill, particularly one where vineyards are cultivated. Also refers to an upland region or a divide between two river valleys.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for holding the "high ground" or for a place where success is cultivated (like a vineyard). Represents an elevated position or a turning point between two paths.

## Syllables:
2 (co-teau)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
hillside, slope, upland, divide, ridge

## Antonyms:
valley, lowland, depression

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: show, go, slow / SLANT: photo, motto, total / MULTI: coteau height, end the night, shining bright

2. Cultivating greatness, coteau for the few.
3. High ground status, coteau through and through.

## 3 punchline angles:

2. Cultivating success (vineyard link)
