# WORD: Zephyr

## Meaning (plain):
A gentle, mild breeze; also refers to the west wind (Zephyrus in Greek mythology) or fine, light fabric.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a light, smooth flow that travels easily; a "breath of fresh air" in the game or a calm energy.

## Syllables:
2 (ze-phyr)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
breeze, air, wind, breath, draft

## Antonyms:
storm, gale, hurricane

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: heifer, lever / SLANT: ever, never / MULTI: Zephyr in the sky, watch the clouds fly, never say die

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Smooth flow, gentle like a Zephyr.
2. West wind blowing, call me the Zephyr.
3. Light on my feet, moving with the Zephyr.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Smooth/Gentle" flow vs Harsh/Aggressive
2. "West Wind" (Zephyrus) mythology
3. "Fine Fabric" (Luxury/Quality)
