# WORD: Alibris

## Meaning (plain):
An online retail marketplace for new, used, and rare books, as well as movies and music.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being a source of rare knowledge, "classic" status, or having an extensive catalog of work. Like a library or marketplace for the gems others can't find.
