# WORD: Meed

## Meaning (plain):
(Archaic/Literary) A deserved share, reward, or recompense; what someone has earned through their labor or character.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the literal "payout" of the game. Represents the status, money, and respect that you have rightfully earned through years of struggle. Carrying your "meed" means you are finally reaping the rewards of your character.

reward, recompense, share, earnings, payment

## Antonyms:
