# WORD: Zephyrless

## Meaning (plain):
Without a zephyr; lacking a gentle breeze or light wind; completely still or stagnant air.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "dead calm" or stagnation; no movement in the game, or a suffocating, airless environment.

## Syllables:
3 (ze-phyr-less)

## Part of speech:
Adjective

## Synonyms:
still, stagnant, airless, breathless

## Antonyms:
breezy, windy, zephyry

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: effortless, breathless, reckless / SLANT: mess, chess / MULTI: Zephyrless state, trapped in the gate, decide the fate

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. The air is Zephyrless, wait for the storm.
2. Stagnant energy, feeling Zephyrless.
3. No wind in the sails, completely Zephyrless.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Dead Calm" (Stagnation/Lack of growth)
2. Suffocating/Airless pressure
3. Silence before the storm

## Tags:
nature, air, stagnant, calm
