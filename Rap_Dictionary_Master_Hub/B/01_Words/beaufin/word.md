# WORD: Beaufin

## Meaning (plain):
1. An archaic form of "biffin," which is a type of cooking apple from Norfolk, England. 2. A commune in south-eastern France.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being a "sweet but firm" element in the scene. Represents a vintage, sophisticated flavor that is rooted in a specific place (France or Norfolk).
