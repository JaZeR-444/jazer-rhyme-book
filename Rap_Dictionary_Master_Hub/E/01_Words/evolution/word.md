# WORD: Evolution

## Meaning (plain):
descent with modification from preexisting species : cumulative inherited change in a population of organisms through time leading to the appearance of new forms : the process by which new species or populations of living things develop from preexisting forms through successive generations; also : the scientific theory explaining the appearance of new species and varieties through the action of various biological mechanisms (such as natural selection, genetic mutation or drift, and hybridization); the historical development of a biological group (such as a species) : phylogeny; a process of change in a certain direction : unfolding
