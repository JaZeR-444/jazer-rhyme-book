# WORD: Estreat

## Meaning (plain):
1. A true copy or extract of an original record, especially of a fine or amercement to be levied. 2. To extract from the records of a court so as to enforce or prosecute.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for an enforced penalty or a "final record" of a rival's failure. Represents the moment when the "debt" is officially recorded and collected. A permanent extract of one's status.
