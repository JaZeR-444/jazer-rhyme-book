# WORD: Zillionaire

## Meaning (plain):
An informal term for a person of incalculably or immeasurably great wealth.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing top-tier riches, having "uncountable" money, or being at the absolute peak of the wealth ladder.

## Syllables:
3 (zil-lion-aire)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
billionaire, multimillionaire, tycoon, wealthy person

## Antonyms:
pauper, beggar, broke

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: flare, stare, snare, lair / SLANT: billion, stallion / MULTI: Zillionaire status, watch 'em hate us, nobody can rate us

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Living life like a young Zillionaire.
2. Counting up the racks, call me Zillionaire.
3. Beyond the millions, I'm a real Zillionaire.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Uncountable Wealth" (Exaggerated riches)
2. "Zillionaire/Hate us" (Status vs Envy)
3. Higher than Billionaire (Superiority)

## Tags:
money, wealth, status, slang
