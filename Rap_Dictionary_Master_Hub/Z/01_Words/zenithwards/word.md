# WORD: Zenithwards

## Meaning (plain):
Moving or directed toward the zenith; upward toward the highest point.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Always aiming higher, the "upward trajectory" of the career, or looking straight to the top.

## Syllables:
3 (ze-nith-wards)

## Part of speech:
Adverb

## Synonyms:
upward, topward, skyward

## Antonyms:
downward, nadirwards

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: guards, cards, yards / SLANT: limits, spirits / MULTI: Zenithwards bound, never looking down, king of the town

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Moving Zenithwards, I'm reaching for the crown.
2. My trajectory is Zenithwards, never coming down.
3. Aim the vision Zenithwards, past the clouds.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Upward Trajectory" (Constant growth)
2. Aiming for the highest possible point
3. Upward movement vs the "downward" spiral of others

## Tags:
movement, direction, success, peak
