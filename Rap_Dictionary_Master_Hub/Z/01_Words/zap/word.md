# WORD: Zap

## Meaning (plain):
To attack or destroy quickly with electricity or force; to move or send quickly; to cook in a microwave.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Quick disposal of an opponent, a burst of energy, or "heating up" a track instantly.

## Syllables:
1 (zap)

## Part of speech:
Verb/Noun

## Synonyms:
shock, hit, heat, move, flash

## Antonyms:
slow, freeze, preserve

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: cap, map, lap, trap / SLANT: bag, tag / MULTI: Zap 'em with the flow, let the energy go, watch the crowd grow

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. One bar, then I zap 'em, they don't even know.
2. Heat it up, zap the track, let the power show.
3. Move quick, zap, I'm already at the top.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Microwaving" the track (heating it up instantly)
2. Electric shock/Laser precision
3. Quick disposal/Flash movement

## Tags:
energy, attack, quick, electricity
