# WORD: Biethnic

## Meaning (plain):
Involving, relating to, or belonging to two distinct ethnicities.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a dual-heritage flow or for navigating between two different worlds. Represents a versatile identity that can speak to multiple cultures and backgrounds at once.

## Syllables:
3 (bi-eth-nic)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
dual-heritage, mixed, multi-ethnic, bi-cultural

## Antonyms:
monoethnic, uniform

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: metric, electric, hectic / SLANT: ethics, sceptic, hectic / MULTI: biethnic roots, different boots, cultural shoots
