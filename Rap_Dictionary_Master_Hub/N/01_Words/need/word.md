# WORD: Need

## Meaning (plain):
To require something because it is essential or very important; a situation in which someone must do or have something; a lack of something necessary.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being the "missing piece" of the game. Represents the realization that the culture is "lacking" something until you arrive to provide it. You are the essential "requirement" that makes the whole scene complete.
