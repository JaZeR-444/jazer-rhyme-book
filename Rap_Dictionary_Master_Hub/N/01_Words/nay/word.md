# WORD: Nay

## Meaning (plain):
(Archaic/Literary) No; a negative answer or vote; used to introduce a more intense or surprising statement.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for "refusal" and uncompromising standards. When you say "nay," you're rejecting the common trends and the weak offers of the industry. Represents a negative vote against the fake and a push toward something more intense.
