# WORD: Artillery

## Meaning (plain):
weapons (such as bows, slings, and catapults) for discharging missiles; large bore mounted firearms (such as guns, howitzers, and rockets) : ordnance; especially : such ordnance that is capable of long-range indirect fire at a target too distant to be seen; a branch of an army armed with artillery
