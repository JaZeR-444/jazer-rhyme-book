# WORD: Abandon

## Meaning (plain):
To give up completely; to desert or forsake; to yield oneself to an impulse or emotion.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for leaving behind all restraint and going "all in." Represents a flow that is wild, uncontrolled, and fully committed to the moment without fear of consequences.

## Syllables:
3 (a-ban-don)

## Part of speech:
verb / noun

## Synonyms:
desert, forsake, surrender, yield, relinquish

## Antonyms:
keep, maintain, restrain, control

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: don, dawn, gone / SLANT: phantom, tandem, random / MULTI: abandon the scene, go for the dream, nothing between

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Spitting with abandon, watch the whole world awake.
2. I left 'em in abandon, every rule that I break.
3. Total abandon, giving everything it takes.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Going "all in" without restraint
2. Deserting/leaving the fake behind
3. Surrendering to the flow/impulse

## Tags:
attitude, movement, common
