# WORD: Betide

## Meaning (plain):
(Archaic) To happen or occur; to take place by fate or fortune. Often used in the phrase "woe betide."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the inevitability of your success or the "fate" that awaits your rivals. Represents an event or outcome that is destined to happen regardless of what anyone else does.

## Syllables:
2 (be-tide)

## Part of speech:
verb

## Synonyms:
happen, occur, befall, chance

## Antonyms:
stop, prevent, avoid

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: ride, hide, side / SLANT: decide, divide, reside / MULTI: woe betide, nowhere to hide, turn the tide

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Woe betide the one who tries to block the path.
2. Success will betide me, feel the cosmic wrath.
3. Whatever may betide, I'm staying on my side.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Inevitability of success/fate
2. "Woe betide" threats to rivals
3. Destined outcomes

## Tags:
archaic, unique, power, attitude
