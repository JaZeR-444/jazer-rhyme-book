# WORD: Akaakai

## Meaning (plain):
The Hawaiian name for the great bulrush (Schoenoplectus californicus), a plant used for weaving and building. It can also refer to a type of wild onion.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for something that is naturally woven into the culture or something that adds a "sharp" layer like an onion. Represents island roots and natural utility.
