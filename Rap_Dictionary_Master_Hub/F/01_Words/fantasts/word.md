# WORD: Fantasts

## Meaning (plain):
Visionaries or dreamers; people who indulge in fantasies or predict the future through imagination.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for having a high-level creative vision that the average person can't grasp. Represents those who see the future of the game and "dream" up new styles and concepts before they exist.

## Syllables:
2 (fan-tasts)

## Part of speech:
noun (plural)

## Synonyms:
visionaries, dreamers, imaginers, futurists

## Antonyms:
realists, pragmatists, cynics

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: blasts, casts, fasts / SLANT: tasks, asks, masks / MULTI: fantasts dream, beyond the scheme, out of the stream

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. We the fantasts, dreaming up the new design.
2. Visionary energy, fantasts on the line.
3. Beyond the reality, fantasts with the sign.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. High-level/futuristic vision
2. Imagination vs. Reality
3. Leading through creative "dreams"
