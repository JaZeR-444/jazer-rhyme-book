# WORD: Zigzagway

## Meaning (plain):
An erratic path or course; in military context, describes erratic movements; in Ayurveda, refers to a disruption in flow.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "disrupted" or "erratic" way of life; moving in a way that breaks the balance or confuses the enemy.

## Syllables:
3 (zig-zag-way)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
erratic path, winding way, detour

## Antonyms:
straightway

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: day, stay, play, pay / SLANT: highway, skyway / MULTI: Zigzagway life, through the fire and strife, sharp like a knife

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Navigating the struggle, that's the Zigzagway.
2. Erratic tactics, moving in a Zigzagway.
3. No balance in the flow, purely Zigzagway.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Erratic Tactics" (Confusing the opposition)
2. "Disruption of Flow" (Unconventional rhythm)
3. Non-linear life journey
