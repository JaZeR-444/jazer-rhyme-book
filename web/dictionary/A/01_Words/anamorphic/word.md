# WORD: Anamorphic

## Meaning (plain):
A cinematic term for a distorted or widescreen perspective. Represents seeing life in a grander, more epic format than the standard view.

## Sonic Texture:
Cinematic, Spacious.

## Contrast:
vs. Standard / Narrow.
