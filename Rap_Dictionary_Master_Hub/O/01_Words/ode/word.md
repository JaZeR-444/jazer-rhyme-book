# WORD: Ode

## Meaning (plain):
A formal, ceremonious lyric poem that addresses and often celebrates a person, place, thing, or idea.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "tribute" or a high-end celebratory track. Writing an "ode" to the streets or to a legend means you're creating a formal, lyrical masterpiece that honors the subject with serious weight and ceremony. A track of high respect.

## Syllables:
1 (ode)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
poem, lyric, song, tribute, hymn, eulogy

## Antonyms:
satire, insult, diss-track

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: road, code, load / SLANT: mode, showed, owed / MULTI: street ode, purely the code, on the road
