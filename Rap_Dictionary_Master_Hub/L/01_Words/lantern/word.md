# WORD: Lantern

## Meaning (plain):
A portable protective case for a light with transparent openings; a lamp in a clear case with a handle.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being a "guiding light" in the dark or for carrying a protected, portable brilliance. Represents someone who illuminates the way for others while keeping their own "fire" safe from the elements.

## Syllables:
2 (lan-tern)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
lamp, beacon, guide, light, signal

## Antonyms:
shadow, darkness, obscurity

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: turn, burn, learn / SLANT: pattern, modern, western / MULTI: lantern light, end the night, shining bright

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Carrying the lantern, I'm the light in the dark.
2. Protected brilliance, leaving a lantern mark.
3. Guide 'em through the tunnel, lantern in the spark.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Being a "guide" for the culture
2. Carrying a "protected" fire/talent
3. Illuminating the truth in dark places

## Tags:
light, common, unique, guidance
