# WORD: Amolish

## Meaning (plain):
1. (Rare/Archaic) To put away, remove, or get rid of (from Latin *amoliri*). 2. A common misspelling or blend of "abolish" and "demolish."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
A high-intensity term for completely removing or destroying the competition. Combining the "legal" finality of abolish with the "physical" destruction of demolish.

## Syllables:
3 (a-mo-lish)

## Part of speech:
verb

## Synonyms:
destroy, abolish, demolish, remove, eliminate

## Antonyms:
create, establish, build
