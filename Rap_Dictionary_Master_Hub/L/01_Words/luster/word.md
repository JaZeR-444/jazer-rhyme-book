# WORD: Luster

## Meaning (plain):
A glow of reflected light; a sheen, gloss, or brilliant quality; an attractive or distinguished quality.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for having that "high-end glow" or for being so polished that your reputation shines naturally. Represents a distinguished quality that sets you apart from the "dull" competition.

## Syllables:
2 (lus-ter)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
sheen, gloss, brilliance, radiance, sparkle

## Antonyms:
dullness, matte, darkness, obscurity

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: mustard, buster, cluster / SLANT: flustered, trusted, adjusted / MULTI: high end luster, can't be flustered, move with the cluster

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Polished the reputation until it had a natural luster.
2. High-end sheen, a real game-buster with the luster.
3. Distinguished quality, luster in the cluster.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Natural/distinguished brilliance
2. Polished/finished reputation
3. "Shining" above the dull competition

## Tags:
light, status, common, appearance
