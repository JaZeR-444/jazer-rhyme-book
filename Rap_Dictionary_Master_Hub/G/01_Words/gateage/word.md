# WORD: Gateage

## Meaning (plain):
1. The use of gates or the area of a gate opening (as in a turbine or for water control). 2. Money paid to a person being released from prison to help them start their life on the outside.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the "cost" of entry into the game or the resources needed to start fresh after a struggle. Represents the opening of the "gates" to success and the literal "gateage" (funding) that fuels the new beginning.
