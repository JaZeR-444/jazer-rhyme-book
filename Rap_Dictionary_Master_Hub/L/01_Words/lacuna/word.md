# WORD: Lacuna

## Meaning (plain):
A blank space, a missing part, or a gap; specifically in a manuscript, piece of music, or an anatomical structure.

Metaphor for a "gap" in the scene that you are here to fill. Represents a moment of silence or a missing element in the culture that your flow finally provides. Also refers to the "blind spots" of the competition.
