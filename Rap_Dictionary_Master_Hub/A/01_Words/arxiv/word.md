# WORD: Arxiv

## Meaning (plain):
An open-access repository of electronic preprints (known as e-prints) of scholarly articles in the fields of physics, mathematics, computer science, and more. Pronounced "archive."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being the source of original, "pre-print" information or for sharing ideas before they hit the mainstream. Represents intellectual depth and early, exclusive access to knowledge.
