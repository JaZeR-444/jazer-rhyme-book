# WORD: Zelant

## Meaning (plain):
An archaic spelling of "zealant," meaning a person who is enthusiastic, passionate, and unwavering in their commitment to a cause.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Being a "passionate warrior" for the bars; having an old-school, unwavering commitment to excellence.

## Syllables:
2 (ze-lant)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
zealot, enthusiast, fan, fanatic

## Antonyms:
indifferent, apathetic

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: plant, grant, slant, cant / SLANT: silent, violent / MULTI: Zelant soul, take the control, reach the goal

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm a hip-hop Zelant, living for the light.
2. Unwavering passion, spirit of a Zelant.
3. Old-school devotion, call me the Zelant.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Archaic "warrior" feel
2. Unwavering commitment to the craft
3. Passion vs Indifference
