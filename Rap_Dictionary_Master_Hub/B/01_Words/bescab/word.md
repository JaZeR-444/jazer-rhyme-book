# WORD: Bescale

## Meaning (plain):
To scale or climb up; to ascend using a scale or ladder. (Variant or archaic form related to "scale").

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for scaling up your production or climbing the industry ladder. Represents the act of increasing your "scale" (reach or wealth) and ascending to a new level of power.

## Syllables:
2 (be-scale)

## Part of speech:
verb

## Synonyms:
scale, climb, ascend, increase, expand

## Antonyms:
descend, shrink, fall, decrease

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: trail, male, sale / SLANT: detail, retail, prevail / MULTI: bescale the wall, never fall, answer the call

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Scaling up the operation, watch me bescale.
2. Climbing to the top floor, bescale the trail.
3. Every move is larger, bescale without fail.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Scaling up production/reach
2. Climbing the industry ladder
3. Growth and expansion metaphors
