# WORD: Beetfly

## Meaning (plain):
A muscid fly (*Pegomyia hyoscyami*) whose larvae burrow into the leaves of beets and related plants, causing damage.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a persistent "pest" or a "leech" that burrows into the successes of others. Represents someone who tries to "eat" off someone else's harvest or cause damage from the inside.

## Syllables:
2 (beet-fly)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
pest, parasite, leech, leaf-miner

## Antonyms:
provider, protector, ally

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: sky, high, fly / SLANT: night, sight, bright / MULTI: beetfly pest, never rest, in the nest

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Burrowing in the leaves like a common beetfly.
2. Don't let the pest in, watch the beetfly die.
3. Leeches in the scene, beetfly in the green.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Parasitic/pest-like behavior
2. Damaging from the inside
3. Burrowing into someone else's "harvest"
