# WORD: Zaddikim

## Meaning (plain):
Plural of zaddik; the righteous ones in Judaism, often believed to have a special connection to God.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The "chosen few," the council of righteous leaders, or the collective of saintly figures.

## Syllables:
3 (zad-di-kim)

## Part of speech:
Noun (Plural)

## Synonyms:
tzaddikim, righteous ones, holy council

## Antonyms:
wicked ones, sinners

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: slim, grim, hymn / SLANT: rhythm, system / MULTI: Zaddikim dream, stay on the team, pure like the stream

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Surrounded by the Zaddikim, we move in silence.
2. The Zaddikim know the path, we avoid the violence.
3. Seeking the truth with the Zaddikim.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. The power of a righteous collective
2. Connection to the divine/prophecy
3. Standing among the "righteous ones"
