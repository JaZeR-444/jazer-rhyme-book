# WORD: Ultimate

## Meaning (plain):
Happening or coming at the end of a process; final or conclusive; highest in degree or order; the best or most extreme example of something.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The ultimate status in the game—being at the absolute end-point of success. Represents someone who is the final, conclusive answer to the question of "who is the best." Carrying the energy of the extreme example—the one who can't be surpassed.

## Syllables:
3 (ul-ti-mate)

## Part of speech:
adjective / noun

## Synonyms:
final, conclusive, highest, best, extreme, utmost

## Antonyms:
initial, beginning, mediocre, lowest

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: state, fate, great / SLANT: deliberate, legitimate, intimate / MULTI: purely ultimate, watch the state, decide the fate
