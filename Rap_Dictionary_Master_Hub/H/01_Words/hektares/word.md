# WORD: Hektares

## Meaning (plain):
A variant spelling of hectares; a metric unit of area equal to 10,000 square meters (approximately 2.471 acres). Primarily used for land measurement.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for measuring "massive" success and owning large territories. Represents someone whose influence and wealth are so big they have to be measured in hectares, not just small plots.

2 (hek-tares)
