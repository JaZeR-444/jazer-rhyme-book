# WORD: Hacks

## Meaning (plain):
1. Strategies or techniques for efficient use of time or resources (life hacks). 2. Unauthorized access to computer systems. 3. (Informal) People who do unoriginal or mediocre work.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for disrupting the system or for having the "short-cuts" to success that others don't know. Can also refer to exposing the "hacks" (fake or mediocre rappers) in the game. Represents a high-level technical disruption.

## Syllables:
1 (hacks)

## Part of speech:
noun (plural) / verb

## Synonyms:
disruptions, short-cuts, unoriginal-work, exploits, cracks

## Antonyms:
originals, masters, protocol, standard-way

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: stacks, tracks, back / SLANT: fax, tax, wax / MULTI: life hacks, big stacks, on the tracks

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm hacking the system, found the glitch in the code.
2. No hacks here, just the original mode.
3. Exposing the hacks while I'm building the road.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Disrupting/hacking the industry
2. Exposing unoriginal "hacks" (rivals)
