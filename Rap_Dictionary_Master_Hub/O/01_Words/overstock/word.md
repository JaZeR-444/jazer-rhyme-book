# WORD: Overstock

## Meaning (plain):
To stock too much or too many of something; to have an excessively large inventory of goods; a surplus of stock.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for having a "surplus of heat." Represents someone who has so many hits and so much talent in the "vault" that they can't even drop it all. Carrying an inventory of power that exceeds the market's requirement.

## Syllables:
3 (o-ver-stock)

## Part of speech:
noun / verb

## Synonyms:
surplus, excess, inventory, stockpile, abundance

## Antonyms:
shortage, vacuum, scarcity, lack

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: block, clock, lock / SLANT: hawk, walk, talk / MULTI: massive overstock, on the block, wind the clock

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I got that overstock, the vault is overflowing with heat.
2. Surplus of the rhythm, overstock in the street.
3. Every move is a quantity, overstock for the nation.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Surplus of hits/talent
2. Having "too much" to give
3. Excess inventory of power
