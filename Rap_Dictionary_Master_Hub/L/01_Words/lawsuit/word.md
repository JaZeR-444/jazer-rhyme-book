# WORD: Lawsuit

## Meaning (plain):
A legal action brought before a civil court in which a plaintiff seeks a remedy for a dispute with a defendant.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a formal, high-stakes "clash" or beef that is being settled in the eyes of the public. Represents a verbal "court case" where the winner takes the reputation and the loser pays the price. A serious, documented dispute.

2 (law-suit)

## Part of speech:

litigation, case, action, dispute, trial

## Antonyms:
