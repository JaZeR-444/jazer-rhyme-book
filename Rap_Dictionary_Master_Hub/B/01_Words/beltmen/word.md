# WORD: Beltmen

## Meaning (plain):
1. Plural of beltman; a worker responsible for inspecting and maintaining machine belts. 2. (Australian/NZ) A lifeguard who swims with a safety line attached to their belt.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being the "maintenance crew" that keeps the industry machine running or the "lifeline" for the team in dangerous waters. Represents the essential, often behind-the-scenes strength.
