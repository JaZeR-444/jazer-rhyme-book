# WORD: Zulkadah

## Meaning (plain):
An alternative spelling of Dhu al-Qadah, the eleventh month of the Islamic lunar calendar; a sacred month where fighting is forbidden.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "sacred peace" or a period of truce; having a holy, calm energy that commands respect and stops the conflict.

## Syllables:
3 (zul-ka-dah)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
