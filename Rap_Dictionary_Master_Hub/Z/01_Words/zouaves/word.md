# WORD: Zouaves

## Meaning (plain):
Members of a former body of French infantry, originally Algerians, distinguished by their colorful uniforms, hardiness, and dash.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing "hardy and dashing" warriors; having a colorful, elite style that masks a hardened, battle-ready interior.

## Syllables:
2 (zou-aves)

## Part of speech:
Noun (Plural)

## Synonyms:
warriors, soldiers, elite infantry, dashers

## Antonyms:
cowards, pacifists

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: waves, saves, caves, graves / SLANT: motor, rotor / MULTI: Zouaves in the heat, never know defeat, ruling the street

2. Hardy in the battle, spirit of the Zouaves.
3. Dashing through the fire, purely Zouaves.
