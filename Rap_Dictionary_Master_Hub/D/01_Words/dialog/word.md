# WORD: Dialog

## Meaning (plain):
the conversational element of literary or dramatic composition (such as a movie, play, or novel); a conversation between two or more persons; also : a similar exchange between a person and something else (such as a computer) —usually used before another noun; an exchange of ideas and opinions
