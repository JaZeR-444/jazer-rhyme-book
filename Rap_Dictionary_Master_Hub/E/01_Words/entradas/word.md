# WORD: Entradas

## Meaning (plain):
1. (Spanish) An expedition or journey into unexplored territory, particularly historical Spanish expeditions in the Americas. 2. Entrance, entry, or tickets for an event.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for an "expedition" into new musical territory or for having the "ticket" (entry) to the exclusive clubs and high-end scene. Represents a pioneering spirit and exclusive access.
