# WORD: Headrail

## Meaning (plain):
1. An elliptical rail at a wooden ship's head extending from the figurehead to the bow. 2. A boxlike case across the top of a window blind covering the mechanical devices. 3. (Billiards) The end of the table from which play starts.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being at the "head" of the ship (leadership) or for "covering" the mechanics behind the scenes. Represents someone who leads the voyage and keeps the inner workings hidden from the public.
