# WORD: Decene

## Meaning (plain):
An organic compound (alkene) with the chemical formula C10H20, consisting of a chain of ten carbon atoms with one double bond. Used industrially in synthetic lubricants.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "double-bonded" high-intensity connection or a flow that is slick like a synthetic lubricant. Represents a strong, industrial-grade chain of bars that keeps the machine running.
