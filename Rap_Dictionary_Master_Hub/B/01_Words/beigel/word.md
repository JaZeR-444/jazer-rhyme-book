# WORD: Beigel

## Meaning (plain):
1. A variant spelling of "bagel," a ring-shaped bread roll that is boiled before being baked. 2. (Tennis Slang) A set in which one player wins all six games (6-0).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a valuable "closed circle" (a bagel) or for a total shutout (winning 6-0). Represents being in a high-value group or completely dominating a "set" or situation.
