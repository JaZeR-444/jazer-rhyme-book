# WORD: Zigzagline

## Meaning (plain):
A line characterized by sharp turns in alternating directions, resembling a series of the letter 'Z'.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a non-linear journey; a path that takes sharp turns to avoid the expected route.
