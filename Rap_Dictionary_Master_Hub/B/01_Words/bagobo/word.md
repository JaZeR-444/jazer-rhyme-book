# WORD: Bagobo

## Meaning (plain):
An indigenous ethnic group from southern Mindanao in the Philippines. The name is derived from "bago" (new) and "obo" (growth).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for "new growth" and indigenous resilience. Represents a group or persona that has recently formed but has deep, cultural roots and an unbreakable spirit.
