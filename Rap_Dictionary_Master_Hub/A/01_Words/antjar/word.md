# WORD: Antjar

## Meaning (plain):
The upas tree of Java (Antiaris toxicaria) or the virulent poison prepared from its milky gum resin, historically used on arrows.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a lethal, natural poison. A "venomous" flow that is silent, effective, and derived from a "forbidden" source. Represents a strike that ends the target immediately.
