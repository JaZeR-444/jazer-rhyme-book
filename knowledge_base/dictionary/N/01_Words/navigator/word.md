# WORD: Navigator

## Meaning (plain):
A person who plans and directs the route of a ship, aircraft, or other transport; someone who find the way.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being the "pilot" of the crew or the culture. Represents the leader who sees the path ahead and directs everyone else through the "deep waters" of the industry. The one who keeps the movement on course.

## Syllables:
4 (nav-i-ga-tor)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
pilot, guide, leader, director, steersman

## Antonyms:
follower, drifter, lost-one

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: creator, hater, greater / SLANT: nature, paper, player / MULTI: chief navigator, watch the hater, high-end creator

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm the chief navigator, steering the whole team.
2. Pilot of the movement, navigator in the dream.
3. Every verse is a map, navigator for the stream.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Being the "pilot" or leader
2. Seeing the "path" to success before others
3. Keeping the movement on course
