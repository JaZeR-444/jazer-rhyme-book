# WORD: Zambezi

## Meaning (plain):
The fourth-longest river in Africa, flowing into the Indian Ocean; its name means "Great River."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a powerful, unstoppable flow, a "Great River" of talent, or deep African roots.
