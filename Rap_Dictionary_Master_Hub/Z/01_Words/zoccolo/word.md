# WORD: Zoccolo

## Meaning (plain):
An Italian word meaning "hoof," "clog" (wooden shoe), or "plinth" (architectural base).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a hard, "hoof-like" stomp or being the solid base of the structure; the "clog" that walks over the competition.

## Syllables:
3 (zoc-co-lo)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
hoof, clog, plinth, base, shoe

## Antonyms:
top, roof

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: solo, polo, photo / SLANT: ghetto, meadow / MULTI: Zoccolo stomp, in the swamp, hear the chomp

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Hard stomp on the stage, feel the Zoccolo.
2. Solid architectural base, I'm the Zoccolo.
3. Walking in wooden shoes, pure Zoccolo.

## 3 punchline angles:
