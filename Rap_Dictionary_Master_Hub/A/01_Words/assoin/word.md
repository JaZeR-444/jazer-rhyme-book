# WORD: Assoin

## Meaning (plain):
An archaic variant or alteration of "essoin," meaning an excuse for not appearing in court at the time appointed.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for an excuse or a delay in showing up. Refers to "dodging" a confrontation or having a valid (but archaic) reason for being absent from the scene.

## Syllables:
2 (as-soin)

## Part of speech:
noun / verb

## Synonyms:
essoin, excuse, delay, alibi

## Antonyms:
appearance, presence, confrontation

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: join, coin, groin / SLANT: noise, poise, boys / MULTI: assoin plea, dodgin' the fee, wait and see

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. No assoin today, I'm showing up in force.
2. They looking for an assoin to change the whole course.
3. Every absence an assoin, excuses from the source.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Dodging/excusing absences
2. Legal/formal delay tactics
3. Confrontation avoidance
