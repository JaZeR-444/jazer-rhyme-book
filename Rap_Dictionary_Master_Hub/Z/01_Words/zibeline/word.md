# WORD: Zibeline

## Meaning (plain):
Of or relating to the sable (a pine marten); also refers to the fur of the sable or a thick, soft woolen cloth made to resemble it.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing top-tier luxury, wearing the "sable" (wealth/status), or having a smooth, velvety flow.
