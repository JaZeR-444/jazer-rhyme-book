# WORD: Halo

## Meaning (plain):
A circle of light shown around or above the head of a saint or holy person; a luminous ring or disk.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for divine status or for having an unshakeable, "holy" presence in the scene. Represents someone who is seen as a savior or a light-bearer for the culture. Carrying an energy that is both bright and untouchable.

## Syllables:
2 (ha-lo)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
nimbus, ring, light, aura, crown

## Antonyms:
shadow, darkness, curse

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: show, go, slow / SLANT: solo, photo, hollow / MULTI: halo shine, purely divine, stay in line

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm moving with a halo, untouchable and bright.
2. Luminous energy, halo in the night.
3. Crown made of light, call it a halo tonight.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Divine/saint-like status
2. Being a "light-bearer" for the culture
3. Luminous/unshakeable aura
