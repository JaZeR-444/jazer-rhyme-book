# WORD: Meditation

## Meaning (plain):
The action or practice of meditating; a discourse intended to express its author's reflections or to guide others in contemplation.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the "disciplined act" of refining the art. Represents the process of constant reflection and mental training that keeps the flow sharp. A track can be a "meditation" if it's deeply personal and reflective.
