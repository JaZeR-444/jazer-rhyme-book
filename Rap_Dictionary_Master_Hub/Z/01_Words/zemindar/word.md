# WORD: Zemindar

## Meaning (plain):
A historical landholder or hereditary tax collector in India, often owning large areas of land rented to farmers.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "landlord" of the game, collecting the "tax" (respect/royalties), or being an elite landholder.

## Syllables:
3 (ze-min-dar)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
landholder, landlord, tax collector, zamindar

## Antonyms:
tenant, peasant
