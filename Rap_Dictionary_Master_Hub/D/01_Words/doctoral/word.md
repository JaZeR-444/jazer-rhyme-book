# WORD: Doctoral

## Meaning (plain):
a person skilled or specializing in healing arts; especially : someone (such as a physician, dentist, or veterinarian) who holds an advanced degree and is licensed to practice; medicine man; an eminent theologian declared a sound expounder of doctrine by the Roman Catholic Church —called also doctor of the church
