# WORD: Afterglow

## Meaning (plain):
Light or radiance remaining in the sky after the sun has set; pleasant feeling after an experience.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The lingering vibe of success or a moment.

## Syllables:
3 (af-ter-glow)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
Twilight, dusk, radiance, remnants

## Antonyms:
Darkness, gloom

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: blow, crow, flow, glow, grow, know, low, row, show, slow, snow, throw, toe
SLANT: go, so, though
MULTI: after show, let it go

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Bask in the afterglow, watch the money flow.
2. The city in the afterglow, a cinematic show.
3. Chasing the afterglow, moving fast then slow.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Sunset/light imagery
2. Legacy/memory
3. Persistence

## Tags:
mood, vibe, light
