# WORD: Bespend

## Meaning (plain):
(Archaic) To spend entirely; to bestow, employ, or consume fully.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for "leaving it all on the track" or for fully bestowing your talent upon the world. Represents an exhaustive commitment where every drop of energy and resource is used.

## Syllables:
2 (be-spend)

## Part of speech:
verb

## Synonyms:
expend, bestow, consume, employ, exhaust

## Antonyms:
save, hoard, conserve, hold-back

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: trend, end, send / SLANT: defend, pretend, blend / MULTI: bespend it all, never stall, answer the call

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I bespend the talent until the cup is dry.
2. Fully employed, watch the bespend fly.
3. No saving the energy, I bespend until I die.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Exhausting all resources/talent
2. Total commitment to the craft
3. Bestowing greatness upon the audience

## Tags:
archaic, unique, intensity
