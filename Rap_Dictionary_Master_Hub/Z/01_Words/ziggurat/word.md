# WORD: Ziggurat

## Meaning (plain):
An ancient Mesopotamian temple tower consisting of a lofty pyramidal structure built in successive stages with a shrine at the top.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "stairway to the gods," building success layer by layer, or having an ancient, towering presence.
