# WORD: Zephyry

## Meaning (plain):
Like a zephyr; gentle, mild, and breezy.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Having a breezy, effortless style; being "light" but present in the atmosphere.

## Syllables:
3 (ze-phyr-y)

## Part of speech:
Adjective

## Synonyms:
breezy, airy, light, gentle

## Antonyms:
stormy, heavy, thick

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: theory, weary, cherry / SLANT: clearly, nearly / MULTI: Zephyry flow, watch the garden grow, let 'em know
