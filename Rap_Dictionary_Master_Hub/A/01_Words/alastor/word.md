# WORD: Alastor

## Meaning (plain):
In Greek mythology, an avenging spirit or deity that pursues those who have committed crimes. Later, it became a general term for an avenger or a demon associated with possessed individuals.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for an unstoppable avenger or a spirit of retribution. Used to describe someone who settles scores or brings a haunting, vengeful energy to a track.
