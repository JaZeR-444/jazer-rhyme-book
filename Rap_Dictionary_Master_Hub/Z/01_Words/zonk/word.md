# WORD: Zonk

## Meaning (plain):
As a verb, to hit someone hard, to fall soundly asleep, or to relax completely; as a noun, an unfavorable prize in a game show.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "knockout" hit (hard bar) or dismissing an opponent as a "bad prize" (worthless).

## Syllables:
1 (zonk)

## Part of speech:
Verb/Noun

## Synonyms:
hit, knockout, sleep, dud, loser

## Antonyms:
win, prize, awake

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: honk, bonk, conk, monk / SLANT: dark, mark / MULTI: Zonk in the ring, hear the bells ring, victory sing

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. One bar, then I zonk 'em, they out for the count.
2. You just a zonk, a bad prize in the amount.
3. Hard hit energy, purely Zonk.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Knockout Blow" (Power of the bars)
2. "Unfavorable Prize" (Dismissing the competition)
3. "Zonk/Bonk" (Cartoonish/Impact rhyme)

## Tags:
energy, action, status, power
