# WORD: Bindweed

## Meaning (plain):
A twining or climbing plant from the genera *Convolvulus* or *Calystegia*, often considered a weed because it can quickly choke out other plants.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being a "choker" or an inescapable force that wraps around the competition. Represents someone who spreads quickly and dominates the "garden" by outgrowing everyone else.
