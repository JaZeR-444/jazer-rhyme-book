# WORD: Pachadom

## Meaning (plain):
A variant spelling of pashadom; the office, rank, estate, or territory of a pasha (a high-ranking official in the Ottoman Empire).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for your "elite territory" or the specific "kingdom" you rule in the streets. Represents a high-ranking, authoritative status where you own the estate and control the flow of power. Carrying the energy of a historic, absolute ruler.
