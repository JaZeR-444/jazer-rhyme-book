# WORD: Artcraft

## Meaning (plain):
Skill or proficiency in an art or craft; the successful merging of artistic expression with technical craftsmanship.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the technical skill and dedication required to master the "craft" of rapping. Represents the intersection of raw, creative talent and the disciplined "handiwork" of writing bars.

## Antonyms:
clumsiness, amateurism, neglect
