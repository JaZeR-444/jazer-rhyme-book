## Meaning (plain):
a low-growing, erect, Eurasian shrub (Vaccinium myrtillus) having blue or bluish-black fruits that resemble blueberries but are borne singly rather than in clusters; the sweet, edible fruit of the bilberry used especially in pies and jams; also : the dried fruit or an extract of this fruit used as an herbal remedy; any of several related plants or their fruit
