# WORD: Zipped

## Meaning (plain):
Past tense of zip; fastened with a zipper; moved very fast; or compressed into a smaller computer file.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing "case closed" (zipped shut), having moved so fast they missed it, or being a "concentrated" (compressed) version of pure fire.
