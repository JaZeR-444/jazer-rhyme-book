# WORD: Bajau

## Meaning (plain):
An indigenous ethnic group of maritime Southeast Asia, often called "sea gypsies" or "sea nomads." They are known for their nomadic, seafaring lifestyle.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a nomadic, uncontainable lifestyle and the ability to thrive in "deep waters" where others can't survive. Represents a "sea gypsy" freedom and a connection to the untamed ocean.
