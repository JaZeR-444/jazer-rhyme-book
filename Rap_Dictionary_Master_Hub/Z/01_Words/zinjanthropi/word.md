# WORD: Zinjanthropi

## Meaning (plain):
Plural of Zinjanthropus (now Paranthropus boisei); a species of early hominid whose remains were found in East Africa (Olduvai Gorge).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing "ancient" or "prehistoric" roots; being the "first humans" of the game, coming from the cradle of humanity.
