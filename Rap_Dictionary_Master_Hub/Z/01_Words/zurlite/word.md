# WORD: Zurlite

## Meaning (plain):
An impure variety of melilite (a sorosilicate mineral); occurring in calcareous blocks, often white or green.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "raw and impure" mineral with hidden value; having a rugged but crystalline style that stands out.
