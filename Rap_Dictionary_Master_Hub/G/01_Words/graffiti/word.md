# WORD: Graffiti

## Meaning (plain):
Writing or drawings scribbled, scratched, or sprayed illicitly on a wall or other surface in a public place.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for leaving your "mark" on the streets or for a visual, raw expression of presence. Represents the "tags" and "pieces" of your legacy that are visible to everyone but often misunderstood by the mainstream.

## Syllables:
3 (graf-fi-ti)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
tagging, street-art, mural, mark, scratching

## Antonyms:
N/A

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: city, gritty, pity / SLANT: bitty, committee, witty / MULTI: graffiti tag, paint the bag, never lag

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Marked the city with graffiti, a permanent sign.
2. Raw and visual, graffiti by design.
3. Every verse is a tag, graffiti in the line.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Leaving a permanent mark/tag
2. Raw/illicit street expression
3. Visual representation of identity
