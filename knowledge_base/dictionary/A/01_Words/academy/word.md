# WORD: Academy

## Meaning (plain):
A school or institution for the advancement of art, science, or literature; higher education; an institution for specialized training.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the "school of hard knocks" or for being so advanced that you are the one teaching the game. Represents high-level expertise and the foundational knowledge required to lead the culture.
