# WORD: Zigzaggy

## Meaning (plain):
Resembling or consisting of one or more zigzags; having many sharp, alternating turns.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Having a wild, erratic flow that never stays in a straight line; a "crooked" but rhythmic style.

## Syllables:
3 (zig-zag-gy)

## Part of speech:
Adjective

## Synonyms:
erratic, winding, crooked, jagged

## Antonyms:
straight, linear, direct

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: baggy, shaggy, craggy / SLANT: savvy, happy / MULTI: Zigzaggy path, avoid the wrath, do the math

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Flow is Zigzaggy, they can't catch the rhythm.
2. Erratic movement, style is pure Zigzaggy.
3. Winding through the city, yeah we staying Zigzaggy.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Unpredictable Path" (Style/Success)
2. "Zigzaggy/Baggy" (Style/Clothing rhyme)
3. Winding through the "Streets"

## Tags:
movement, direction, style, geometry
