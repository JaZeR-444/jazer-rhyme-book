# WORD: Zeniths

## Meaning (plain):
Plural of zenith; multiple highest points, culminations, or celestial points directly overhead.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a series of peaks, multiple "top floor" moments, or reaching the pinnacle in every category.

## Syllables:
2 (ze-niths)

## Part of speech:
Noun (Plural)

## Synonyms:
peaks, summits, pinnacles, culminations

## Antonyms:
nadirs, bottoms

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: myths, smiths, withs / SLANT: limits, finishes / MULTI: Zeniths in the sky, reaching so high, never say die

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Reaching multiple Zeniths, one after the other.
2. I've seen the Zeniths of the game, no need to cover.
3. Looking up at the Zeniths, stars in the view.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Multiple Peaks" (Serial success)
2. Culmination of various skills
3. Celestial dominance (Overhead perspective)

## Tags:
peak, power, plural, success
