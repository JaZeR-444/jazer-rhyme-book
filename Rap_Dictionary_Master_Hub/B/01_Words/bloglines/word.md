# WORD: Bloglines

## Meaning (plain):
A former web-based news aggregator that used RSS and Atom feeds to collect and read syndicated content. It was a pioneer in the early "blogging" era.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being a "pioneer" of information or for having your ears to the ground across multiple sources. Represents a "classic" digital knowledge base that paved the way for the modern "feed."
