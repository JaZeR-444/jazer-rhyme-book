# WORD: Twines

## Meaning (plain):
Plural of twine; strong strings or cords made by twisting strands together; to twist or wind around something.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for an "interwoven" flow or a complex set of street connections. Having "twines" means your bars are made of multiple strands of truth twisted together for maximum strength. Represents a career that is tightly wound and impossible to pull apart.

## Syllables:
1 (twines)

## Part of speech:
noun (plural) / verb (third person singular)

## Synonyms:
cords, strands, twists, braids, connections, bindings

## Antonyms:
unravels, untwists, separates, breaks

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: lines, signs, shines / SLANT: minds, finds, binds / MULTI: interwoven twines, purely for the shines, watch the signs

2. Tightly wound energy, twines in the lines.
3. Multiple strands of truth, twines for the youth.
