# WORD: Jadeite

## Meaning (plain):
A hard, translucent, and rare variety of jade (sodium aluminum silicate), highly prized for its beauty and strength, especially in green varieties.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being a "rare gem" that is both beautiful and incredibly tough. Represents a high-value status that is translucent (authentic) but hard as a rock.

## Syllables:
2 (jade-ite)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
jade, gemstone, precious-stone, silicate, treasure

## Antonyms:
worthless, soft, common, dull

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: light, sight, night / SLANT: shake it, make it, take it / MULTI: jadeite shine, from the mine, rare design

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Rare and precious, shining like a piece of jadeite.
2. Hard as a rock, jadeite in the night light.
3. Translucent truth, jadeite in my youth.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Rarity/exclusive value
2. Hardness/toughness of character
3. Authentic/translucent beauty
