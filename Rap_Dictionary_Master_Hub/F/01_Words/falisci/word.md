# WORD: Falisci

## Meaning (plain):
An ancient Italic tribe that lived in northern Lazio, close to the Etruscans and the Romans. They spoke Faliscan, a language closely related to Latin.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for an "ancient brotherhood" or a unique, foundational voice that stands between two powerful forces (like the Romans and Etruscans). Represents a deep, cultural lineage.
