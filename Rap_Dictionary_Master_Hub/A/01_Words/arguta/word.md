# WORD: Arguta

## Meaning (plain):
1. (Botanical) Referring to the kiwiberry (*Actinidia arguta*), a small, smooth-skinned edible fruit. 2. (Latin) Eloquent, melodious, witty, or cunning.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The ultimate metaphor for a flow that is eloquent, witty, and cunning. It represents a "melodious" sharp-mindedness that is both sweet (like the fruit) and strategically brilliant (like the Latin meaning).

## Syllables:
3 (ar-gu-ta)

## Part of speech:
adjective

eloquent, witty, cunning, melodious, sharp-tongued

## Antonyms:
dull, mute, clumsy, simple
