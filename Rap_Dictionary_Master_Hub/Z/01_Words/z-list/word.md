# WORD: Z-list

## Meaning (plain):
An informal term for a group of people considered the least famous or successful in the entertainment industry (below A, B, C lists).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Dismissing the competition as the absolute bottom of the food chain; the lowest rank of "fame."

## Syllables:
2 (z-list)

## Part of speech:
Noun/Adjective

## Synonyms:
bottom-tier, obscure, failed, unknown

## Antonyms:
A-list, top-tier, famous

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: missed, fist, wrist, twist / SLANT: interest, spirit / MULTI: Z-list crew, what you gonna do, something brand new

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. You just a Z-list, you ain't even on the map.
2. I'm A-list status, while you stuck on the Z-list.
3. Don't mention my name with that Z-list.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Rank/Tier" dismissal
2. "A-list vs Z-list" (Superiority)
3. "Obscurity" (Nobody knows you)

## Tags:
