# WORD: Put

## Meaning (plain):
To move to or place in a particular position; to express something in a particular way.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "single, decisive strike." Represents the moment when you "put" it on 'em—dropping that one bar or that one track that settles everything. Also refers to how you "put" your words together—the specific delivery and expression of your flow.
