# WORD: Zapara

## Meaning (plain):
An indigenous people native to the Amazon rainforest along the border of Ecuador and Peru (also spelled Sápara or Zápara).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing Amazonian indigenous strength, the "voice of the jungle," or a resilient spirit from the rainforest.
