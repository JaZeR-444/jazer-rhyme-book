# WORD: Fab

## Meaning (plain):
1. (Informal) Short for fabulous; extremely good or wonderful. 2. (Tech) Short for fabrication; a factory for manufacturing advanced electronics like silicon chips.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being "manufactured" to perfection or for having high-end, "factory-direct" quality. Represents someone who is not just good, but technically superior and built for high-performance.

## Syllables:
1 (fab)

## Part of speech:
adjective / noun

## Synonyms:
fabulous, excellent, fabrication, plant, factory

## Antonyms:
poor, basic, manual, unpolished

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: lab, slab, grab / SLANT: cab, stab, blab / MULTI: fab lab, high end grab, in the cab

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Fresh from the fab, I'm built for the win.
2. Everything is fab, from the heart to the skin.
3. High performance energy, fab in the spin.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Fabulous/high-end quality
2. Technical/factory-built perfection
3. "Lab to Fab" transformation
