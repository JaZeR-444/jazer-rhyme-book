# WORD: Mediocre

## Meaning (plain):
Of only moderate quality; not very good; ordinary or average.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The ultimate insult for someone who lacks "star power" or extreme talent. Represents being "just okay" in a game that demands excellence. Calling someone mediocre means they are forgettable and have no distinct impact on the scene.

## Syllables:
4 (me-di-o-cre)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
average, ordinary, so-so, common, unremarkable

## Antonyms:
exceptional, superior, legendary, elite

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: joker, poker, smoker / SLANT: speaker, seeker, weaker / MULTI: purely mediocre, life of a joker, stay in the smoker

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. You just a mediocre talent, nobody hears the name.
2. Forgettable rhythm, mediocre in the game.
3. Every move is ordinary, mediocre for the fame.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Being "average" or forgettable
2. Lack of elite talent/impact
3. Mediocre vs. Legendary contrast

## Tags:
attitude, status, common, insult
