# WORD: Faded

## Meaning (plain):
1. Having lost freshness or depth of color. 2. (Slang) Intoxicated by drugs or alcohol.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Often used to describe being high-intensity or completely "out of it" due to success or the lifestyle. Can also describe something that has become outdated or a rival who has lost their "color" and relevance.

## Syllables:
2 (fad-ed)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
intoxicated, high, outdated, withered, dim

## Antonyms:
sober, fresh, vivid, new

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: weighted, hated, created / SLANT: stated, related, updated / MULTI: getting faded, elevated, highly rated

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Feeling elevated, highly faded tonight.
2. Your whole style is faded, losing the light.
3. Stayin' faded in the clouds, out of the sight.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Intoxication/lifestyle "high"
2. Outdated/lost-relevance metaphors
3. "Faded" into the background

## Tags:
slang, status, attitude, common
