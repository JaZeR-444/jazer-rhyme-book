# WORD: Zamarra

## Meaning (plain):
A sheepskin coat or jacket worn by Spanish shepherds.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing rugged, rustic style, "sheep's clothing" (but the real deal), or being a "shepherd" of the flock.
