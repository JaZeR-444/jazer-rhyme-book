# WORD: Bauta

## Meaning (plain):
1. A prehistoric upright memorial stone or gravestone. 2. A specific Venetian mask and costume (half-mask, cape, and tricorn hat) traditionally worn for the Carnival of Venice to hide identity. 3. (Bruneian Slang) Smart and brilliant.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for an immovable "monument" or for having a "brilliant" but hidden identity. Represents someone who is smart and strategic while keeping their true moves behind a mask or a cape.

## Syllables:

monument, mask, disguise, brilliant, smart, memorial
