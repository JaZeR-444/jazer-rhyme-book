# WORD: Larigot

## Meaning (plain):
1. (Music) An organ flute mutation stop sounding two octaves and a fifth above the note played. 2. A small, high-pitched flageolet or flute.

Metaphor for a "piercing" or high-frequency flow. Represents a tone that is shrill, precise, and rises above the bass-heavy noise of the mainstream. A "mutation" that adds a high-end spark to the music.
