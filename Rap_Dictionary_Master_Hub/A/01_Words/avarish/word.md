# WORD: Avarice

## Meaning (plain):
Extreme greed for wealth or material gain; an insatiable desire for profit.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Often used to describe the "hungry" or greedy state of mind required to get to the top. It's that insatiable thirst for more money, more power, and more status that drives the hustle.

## Syllables:
3 (av-a-rice)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
greed, cupidity, covetousness, rapacity, gluttony

## Antonyms:
generosity, philanthropy, charity, selflessness

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: vice, ice, price / SLANT: dice, nice, slice / MULTI: state of avarice, roll of the dice, cold as the ice

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Driven by the avarice, I'm never satisfied.
2. A heart full of avarice, no place for them to hide.
3. Chasin' every dollar, pure avarice in the stride.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Insatiable greed/hunger for success
2. "Vice" of the hustle
3. Wealth accumulation at any cost

## Tags:
attitude, wealth, common
