# WORD: Automatic

## Meaning (plain):
Working by itself with little or no direct human control.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Happening naturally, without thought, or referring to a weapon/machine.

## Syllables:
4 (au-to-mat-ic)

## Part of speech:
Adjective

## Synonyms:
Mechanized, robotic, instinctive, spontaneous

## Antonyms:
Manual, deliberate

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: static, erratic, fanatic, pragmatic, dramatic
SLANT: attic, magic
MULTI: ought to have it, gotta have it
