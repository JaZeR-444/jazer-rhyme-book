# WORD: Laundering

## Meaning (plain):
1. The act of washing and drying clothes. 2. (Illegal) The process of concealing the origin of money obtained from illicit activities by moving it through legitimate systems.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for "cleaning" a reputation or making "street" success look legitimate to the mainstream. Represents the process of taking the raw, "dirty" energy of the hustle and processing it until it looks clean and professional.

## Syllables:
3 (laun-der-ing)

## Part of speech:
noun / verb (present participle)

## Synonyms:
cleaning, washing, processing, concealing, masking

## Antonyms:
exposing, soiling, revealing

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: ring, thing, king / SLANT: pondering, wandering, squandering / MULTI: laundering the fame, part of the game, never the same

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Processing the hustle, laundering the name.
2. Making it look legitimate, laundering the fame.
3. Street energy refined, laundering the game.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Making "dirty" success look clean
2. Concealing the raw origins of wealth/talent
3. Reputation refinement/cleaning
