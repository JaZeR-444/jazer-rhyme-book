# WORD: Lawful

## Meaning (plain):
In harmony with the law; permitted by law; constituted, authorized, or established by law.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for acting with legitimate authority and following the "rules of the game." Represents someone who has the "seal of approval" from the streets or the industry and moves within the sanctioned boundaries of power.

## Syllables:
2 (law-ful)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
legal, legitimate, authorized, permitted, valid

## Antonyms:
illegal, unlawful, prohibited, rogue

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: pull, full, bull / SLANT: awful, thoughtful, mournful / MULTI: lawful right, end the fight, purely bright

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Every move is lawful, authorized by the code.
2. Legitimate power, lawful in the mode.
3. Not a rogue element, lawful on the road.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Legitimate/authorized authority
2. Following the "street code" (lawful)
3. Sanctioned power/status

## Tags:
law, status, common, power
