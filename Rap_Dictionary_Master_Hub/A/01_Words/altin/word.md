# WORD: Altin

## Meaning (plain):
1. A historical gold coin used in the Ottoman Empire and other Turkic states. 2. The word for "gold" in many Turkic languages.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for pure wealth, "gold" standard status, or something of high value and purity. Carrying the weight of historical treasure.

## Syllables:
2 (al-tin)

## Part of speech:
