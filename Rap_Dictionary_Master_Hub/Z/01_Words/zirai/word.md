# WORD: Zirai

## Meaning (plain):
In Swahili, means to faint or lose consciousness; as a name, often of Persian origin meaning "intelligent" or "wise."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "knockout" blow (making 'em faint) or having deep "Zirai" (intelligence/wisdom) in the lyrics.
