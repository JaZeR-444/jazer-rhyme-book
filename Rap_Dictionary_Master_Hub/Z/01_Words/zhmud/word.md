# WORD: Zhmud

## Meaning (plain):
An alternative name for the Samogitians, a subgroup of Lithuanians inhabiting the western part of the country (Zhmud or Samogitia).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing resilient, "Lowland" (Samogitia means lowlands) strength; having a tough, stubborn, and unique Baltic identity.
