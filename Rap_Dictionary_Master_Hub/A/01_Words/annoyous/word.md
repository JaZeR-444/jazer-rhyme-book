# WORD: Annoyous

## Meaning (plain):
(Archaic) Troublesome, annoying, or causing vexation. An older form of the word "annoying."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being a persistent "thorn in the side" of the competition. Carrying a vintage, almost formal weight when describing someone who is troublesome or disruptive to the peace.

## Syllables:
3 (an-noy-ous)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
troublesome, vexatious, annoying, irksome

## Antonyms:
pleasant, soothing, helpful
