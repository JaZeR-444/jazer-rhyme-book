# WORD: Yonder

## Meaning (plain):
At some distance

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Far away, the future, over there, looking ahead

## Syllables:
2 (yon-der)

## Part of speech:
Adverb

## Synonyms:
There, distant

## Antonyms:
Here

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: wander, ponder, squander / SLANT: thunder / MULTI: responder

2. Wild blue yonder, flying private jets
3. From here to yonder, my territory expands

## 3 punchline angles:

3. Future looking
