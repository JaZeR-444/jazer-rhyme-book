# WORD: Bardship

## Meaning (plain):
The office, state, or dignity of a bard (a professional storyteller, verse-maker, or music composer).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The highest level of lyrical status. It represents the official "office" of being a master of words and music. Carrying the dignity and responsibility of a storyteller for the culture.

## Syllables:
2 (bard-ship)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
poethood, mastery, lyricism, storytelling, office

## Antonyms:
amateurism, silence, illiteracy

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: ship, trip, grip / SLANT: hardship, worship, courtship / MULTI: bardship status, raise the lattice, greatest at this

2. Storytelling legacy, bardship from the first.
3. Dignity in the bars, bardship through the worst.
