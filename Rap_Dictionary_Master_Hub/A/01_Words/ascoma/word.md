# WORD: Ascoma

## Meaning (plain):
(Mycology) The multicellular fruiting body of an ascomycete fungus, which contains the asci (spore-bearing cells).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the "fruit" or the visible, peak result of a deep, hidden system (like the underground mycelium). Represents the tangible output of a long, invisible process.
