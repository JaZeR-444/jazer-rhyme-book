# WORD: Zeppelin

## Meaning (plain):
A large dirigible (steerable) airship with a rigid framework; named after Count Ferdinand von Zeppelin.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing "high altitude" status, being a "heavyweight" in the sky, or having a "grand" and classic presence.
