# WORD: Zendic

## Meaning (plain):
An alternative form of "zindiq," referring to a heretic, unbeliever, or one whose beliefs are contrary to dogma.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "heretical" flow that breaks the rules, an outsider who rejects the industry dogma, or a rebel spirit.

## Antonyms:
