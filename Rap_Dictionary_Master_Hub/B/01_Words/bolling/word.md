# WORD: Bolling

## Meaning (plain):
1. A tree from which the branches have been cut back (a pollard). 2. Vigorous or noisy boiling. 3. (Surname) An English name meaning someone with close-cropped hair or a large head.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being "stripped down" to the core (like a tree) or for having a "boiling" high-energy presence. Represents a raw, intense state where all the extra weight has been cut away.
