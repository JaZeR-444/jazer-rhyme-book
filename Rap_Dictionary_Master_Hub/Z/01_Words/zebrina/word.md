# WORD: Zebrina

## Meaning (plain):
A species of creeping plant (Tradescantia zebrina) known for its striped foliage; also called "inch plant" or "wandering dude."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The "wandering" spirit, creeping/growing steadily in the shadows, or having a "variegated" (multi-colored/diverse) style.
