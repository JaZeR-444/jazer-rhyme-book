# WORD: Zoomer

## Meaning (plain):
Informal term for a member of Generation Z (born late 1990s to early 2010s); also refers to someone who uses Zoom software or something that moves fast.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing the "new generation" coming for the throne; having a fast-paced, digitally native energy.

## Syllables:
2 (zoom-er)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
Gen Z, digital native, speedster, newcomer

## Antonyms:
boomer, elder
