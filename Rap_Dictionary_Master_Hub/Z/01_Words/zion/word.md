# WORD: Zion

## Meaning (plain):
A hill in Jerusalem symbolizing the city itself or heaven; metaphorically, an ideal society or the spiritual homeland of believers.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The "promised land" of success, reaching the peak of spirituality, or creating an ideal world for the crew.

## Syllables:
2 (zi-on)

## Part of speech:
Noun (Proper Name)

## Synonyms:
promised land, heaven, utopia, Jerusalem, sanctuary

## Antonyms:
babylon, hell, dystopia
