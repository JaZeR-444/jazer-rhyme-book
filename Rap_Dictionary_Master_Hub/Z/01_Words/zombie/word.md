# WORD: Zombie

## Meaning (plain):
A reanimated corpse in popular culture; or a person who seems only partly alive, without feeling or interest.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Dismissing the competition as "dead inside" or "mindless"; having a hunger that can't be satisfied (eating the game).
