# WORD: Zimbabwe

## Meaning (plain):
A landlocked country in Southeast Africa, formerly known as Southern Rhodesia; known for Great Zimbabwe (ancient ruins) and the Victoria Falls.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing Southern African resilience, the "Great ruins" (ancient stone power), or the "Thunder" of the Zambezi.

## Syllables:
3 (zim-bab-we)

## Part of speech:
Noun (Proper Name)

## Synonyms:
Republic of Zimbabwe, Southern Rhodesia (former)

## Antonyms:
None
