# WORD: Zakah

## Meaning (plain):
An alternative spelling of Zakat; the Islamic practice of almsgiving and one of the Five Pillars of Islam.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Giving back to the community, purifying wealth through charity, or showing spiritual responsibility.

## Syllables:
2 (za-kah)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
Zakat, alms, charity, purification

## Antonyms:
greed, hoarding

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: bra, spa, raw / SLANT: drama, karma / MULTI: Zakah flow, watch the spirit grow, help the people you know

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Purify the wealth, I'm paying my Zakah.
2. Giving to the poor, that's the heart of Zakah.
3. Blessings in the bank because I give my Zakah.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Spiritual purification of "dirty" money
2. Responsibility to the community
3. Growth through giving (etymology: "to grow")
