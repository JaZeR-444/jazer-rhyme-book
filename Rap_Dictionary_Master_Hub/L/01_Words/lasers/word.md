# WORD: Lasers

## Meaning (plain):
Devices that emit a highly coherent beam of light through optical amplification (Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The ultimate metaphor for "precise" focus and high-intensity strikes. Represents someone whose aim, flow, and vision are perfectly aligned and capable of "cutting" through anything with a single beam of truth.

## Antonyms:
