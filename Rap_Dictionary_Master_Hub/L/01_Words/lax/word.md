# WORD: Lax

## Meaning (plain):
Not strict, severe, or careful enough; careless or negligent; loose or slack; not tense or rigid.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being too "loose" or careless with the work. Used to describe someone who has lost their discipline or who is moving with a slack, unpolished energy that leaves them vulnerable.

## Syllables:
1 (lax)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
loose, slack, careless, negligent, remiss

## Antonyms:
strict, rigid, tight, careful, tense

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: tax, facts, tracks / SLANT: fax, wax, max / MULTI: getting lax, pay the tax, end of facts

2. No strict standards, pure lax in the hall.

## 3 punchline angles:
