# WORD: Nerve

## Meaning (plain):
1. (Anatomy) A bundle of fibers that transmits impulses between the brain and body. 2. Courage or boldness in the face of danger or difficulty. 3. A state of nervous agitation.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for "courage" and unshakeable boldness. Having "nerve" means you're not afraid to step into the booth and say exactly what needs to be said, no matter the consequences. It's the physical and mental "wiring" that gives you the strength to stand tall.
