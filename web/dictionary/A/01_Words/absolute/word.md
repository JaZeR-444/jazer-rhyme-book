# WORD: Absolute

## Meaning (plain):
Free from imperfection; perfect; free from restraint or limitation; unconditional; complete and outright.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being at the peak of the game with no exceptions. Represents a flow or status that is undeniable, perfect, and has no "ifs" or "buts" attached. It's the total truth.

## Syllables:
3 (ab-so-lute)

## Part of speech:
adjective / noun

## Synonyms:
complete, total, outright, unconditional, perfect

## Antonyms:
limited, partial, conditional, imperfect

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: flute, loot, route / SLANT: acute, suit, fruit / MULTI: absolute power, final hour, raise the tower

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Denying my status is a lie, my power is absolute.
2. Perfect execution, every move is absolute.
3. No exceptions to the rule, keeping it absolute.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Denying any doubt/exception
2. Being at the "perfect" peak
3. Unconditional power/control

## Tags:
status, power, common
