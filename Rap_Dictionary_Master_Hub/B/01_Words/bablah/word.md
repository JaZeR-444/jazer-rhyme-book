# WORD: Bablah

## Meaning (plain):
The rind of the fruit from certain East Indian species of Acacia (like *Acacia arabica*), which is rich in tannin and used for dyeing and tanning leather.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a transformative process or for "tanning" (toughening up) the exterior. Represents someone who has the raw materials to change or color the scene.
