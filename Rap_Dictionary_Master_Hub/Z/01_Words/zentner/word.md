# WORD: Zentner

## Meaning (plain):
A German unit of mass, traditionally 100 German pounds (approx. 50 kg), but can be 100 kg in some regions.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing "heavy weight," carrying a hundred pounds of pressure, or having a "massive" impact.

## Syllables:
2 (zent-ner)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
hundredweight, quintal, mass, weight
