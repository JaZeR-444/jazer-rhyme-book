# WORD: Jersey

## Meaning (plain):
1. A knitted garment covering the upper body (a sweater). 2. A shirt worn by an athlete during a sports game. 3. A type of slightly elastic, machine-knitted fabric.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for representing your "team" or home turf. Wearing the "jersey" means you are an active player in the game and you carry the identity of your group or city on your back. Represents being part of the starting lineup.

## Syllables:
2 (jer-sey)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
uniform, sweater, kit, fabric, representation

## Antonyms:
N/A

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: sky, city, free / SLANT: mercy, courtesy, earthy / MULTI: jersey on the back, part of the pack, never slack

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Wearing the jersey, representing the whole town.
2. I'm in the starting lineup, jersey never down.
3. Elastic with the flow, jersey in the show.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Representing your team/city
2. Being an "active player" in the game
3. Physical/textural strength (knitted fabric)
