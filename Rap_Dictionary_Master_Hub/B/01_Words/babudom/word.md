# WORD: Babudom

## Meaning (plain):
The world of Indian civil servants or bureaucrats; often used to describe the self-serving, inefficient, or overly complicated nature of a bureaucracy.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for red tape, unnecessary rules, or a system that is designed to slow people down. Represents the "gatekeepers" who use complexity to maintain their own power.

## Syllables:
3 (ba-bu-dom)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
bureaucracy, red tape, officialdom, gatekeeping

## Antonyms:
efficiency, directness, simplicity
