# WORD: Zoom

## Meaning (plain):
To move or travel very quickly; to make a loud low humming sound; or to use a camera lens to make a subject appear closer.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing high-speed movement through the charts or "narrowing the focus" on the prize with precision.

## Syllables:
1 (zoom)

## Part of speech:
Verb/Noun

## Synonyms:
speed, focus, dash, rush, whiz

## Antonyms:
slow, crawl

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: boom, room, doom, gloom, bloom / SLANT: soon, moon / MULTI: Zoom in the lane, no one can restrain, avoid the strain

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I zoom past the haters, they don't even know.
2. Focus the lens, yeah I zoom on the dough.
3. Rapid ascension, watch the spirit Zoom.

## 3 punchline angles:

2. "Narrowing Focus" (Precision/Targeting)
3. "Boom/Zoom" (Impact rhyme)
