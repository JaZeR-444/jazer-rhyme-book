# WORD: Wurmal

## Meaning (plain):
(Archaic) An alternative form of wormil; refers to a warble fly or a grub; a condition characterized by worms or internal struggle.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for an "internal struggle" or a hidden, parasitic threat that eats at your focus. Represents the "worms" of doubt or the rivals who try to infect your movement from the inside out. A status of dark, internal conflict that must be overcome.
