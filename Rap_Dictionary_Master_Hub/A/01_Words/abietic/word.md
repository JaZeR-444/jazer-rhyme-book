# WORD: Abietic

## Meaning (plain):
Relating to abietic acid, a yellowish resin acid obtained from the resin of pine trees and other conifers. It is the primary component of rosin.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Used to describe something derived from the core or resin, signaling a sticky, permanent, or natural impact in a bar.
