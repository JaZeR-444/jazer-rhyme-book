# WORD: Zygopterous

## Meaning (plain):
Of or relating to the Zygoptera (damselflies); characterized by wings that are similar in size and shape and held together over the back at rest.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "balanced and agile" flow; having wings (bars) that are perfectly matched and ready for high-speed maneuvers.
