# WORD: Borassus

## Meaning (plain):
A genus of five species of fan palms (palmyra palms) native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Papua New Guinea. They are economically significant for their timber, fruit, and sap.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "tropical tower" of success. Represents someone who is tall, solid, and provides a lot of "value" (fruit and sap) to their territory. A high-standing, economically powerful force.
