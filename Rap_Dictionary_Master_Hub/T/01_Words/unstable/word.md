# WORD: Unstable

## Meaning (plain):
Not stable; not firm or firmly fixed; unsteady; likely to change suddenly; (psychology) suffering from sudden and extreme changes in mental or emotional state.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "volatile" and dangerous energy. Used to describe someone whose status is shaky or whose mind is so unpredictable that they might strike at any moment. Represents a rival who is falling apart or a situation that is ready to explode into chaos.

## Syllables:
3 (un-sta-ble)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
volatile, shaky, unsteady, variable, erratic, explosive

## Antonyms:
stable, steady, fixed, calm, solid

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: table, cable, label / SLANT: capable, reliable, undeniable / MULTI: street unstable, not a label, watch the cable

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. You looking real unstable, like a house about to fall.
2. Volatile energy, unstable in the hall.
3. Every move is erratic, unstable while I call.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Shaky/failing status of a rival
2. Unpredictable/dangerous energy
3. Unstable vs. Solid/Steady contrast

## Tags:
psychology, unique, status, weakness
