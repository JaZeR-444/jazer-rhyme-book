# WORD: Kavass

## Meaning (plain):
An armed guard or attendant, historically in the service of high-status local or foreign officials in the Ottoman Empire.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being the "armed protector" of the crew or the culture. Represents someone who stands at the gate, high-status and heavily armed, ensuring only the elite can pass.
