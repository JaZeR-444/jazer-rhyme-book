# WORD: Atlantal

## Meaning (plain):
Of or relating to the atlas, the first cervical vertebra of the spine, which supports the head.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being the "support" or the one who carries the weight of the whole system (the head or the empire). Represents a foundational, structural strength that holds everything up.
