# WORD: Awny

## Meaning (plain):
Having awns; specifically, having the bristle-like or hair-like appendages found on the flowering parts of certain grasses and cereals (like barley).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "bristly" or sharp-edged persona. Represents someone who is hard to handle or has a textured, prickly defense that keeps the "soft" competition at a distance.

## Syllables:
2 (awn-y)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
bristly, prickly, barbed, textured, rough

## Antonyms:
smooth, soft, sleek, awnless

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: tawny, brawny, scrawny / SLANT: pony, stony, bony / MULTI: awny flow, brawny show, never let 'em go

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Prickly defense, keepin' it awny and rough.
2. Textured like barley, awny and tough.
3. Don't touch the flow, it's awny with the stuff.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Sharp/prickly personality
2. Hard to handle/defensive texture
3. Natural/rugged strength

## Tags:
nature, unique, attitude
