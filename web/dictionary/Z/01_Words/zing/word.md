# WORD: Zing

## Meaning (plain):
A quality of excitement, vitality, or zest; a sharp piquant flavor; or a humming sound of something moving fast.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The "electric" feeling of a good bar; having that extra pop or sizzle that wakes up the crowd.

## Syllables:
1 (zing)

## Part of speech:

vitality, zest, energy, snap, pop

## Antonyms:
dullness, flatness
