# WORD: Glitter

## Meaning (plain):
To shine brightly with many small flashes of light; small, reflective particles used for decoration.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for superficial brilliance or for having a "flashy" but potentially shallow status. Represents the exterior shine that catches the eye but might lack the "solid gold" core.

## Syllables:
2 (glit-ter)

## Part of speech:
verb / noun

## Synonyms:
sparkle, shimmer, flash, glint, decoration

## Antonyms:
dullness, substance, depth

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: hitter, bitter, litter / SLANT: winter, splinter, center / MULTI: glitter and gold, story untold, never fold

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Not all that glitter is gold in the city.
2. Flashy with the flow, glitter and gritty.
3. Reflective surface, glitter with the pity.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Superficial vs. real value
2. Flashy/decorative status
3. Small flashes of brilliance

## Tags:
light, status, common, appearance
