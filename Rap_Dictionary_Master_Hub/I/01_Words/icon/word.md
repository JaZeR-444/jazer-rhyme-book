# WORD: Icon

## Meaning (plain):
A person or thing regarded as a representative symbol or as worthy of veneration; a sign or representation that stands for something by virtue of a resemblance or analogy to it.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The highest level of status in the game. An icon isn't just a rapper; they are a representative symbol of the whole culture. Being an icon means your image and sound carry a weight that everyone recognizes and respects.

## Syllables:

## Part of speech:

idol, symbol, legend, emblem, figurehead

## Antonyms:
