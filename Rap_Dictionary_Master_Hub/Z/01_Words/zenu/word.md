# WORD: Zenu

## Meaning (plain):
Refers to the goitered antelope (yellow goat); also refers to the Zenú (Sinú) Amerindian tribe of Colombia known for gold work.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing "golden" craftsmanship (Zenú gold), being swift and agile like an antelope, or having South American indigenous roots.
