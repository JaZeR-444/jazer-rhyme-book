# WORD: Zacatons

## Meaning (plain):
Tough, wiry bunchgrasses native to arid regions of the Southwestern US and Mexico (also called sacaton).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Symbol of toughness, surviving in the "arid" (broke/dry) streets, or being "rooted" in the ground.

## Part of speech:
