# WORD: Length

## Meaning (plain):
The measurement or extent of something from end to end; the duration of an event or period of time.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for longevity and the "reach" of your influence. Having "length" means you've been in the game for a long time and your impact extends across the whole scene. It's about how far your bars can travel and how long they stay relevant.

## Syllables:
1 (length)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
extent, duration, reach, span, distance

## Antonyms:
shortness, briefness, proximity

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: strength, tenth, eleventh / SLANT: sense, bench, trench / MULTI: go the length, show the strength, beyond the tenth

1. I'm going the length, watch the legend grow.
2. Reach is extensive, length in the show.
3. Every verse has duration, length for the nation.

## 3 punchline angles:

2. Wide-reaching influence/impact
3. Extent of one's talent/reach
