# WORD: Argentin

## Meaning (plain):
1. Relating to silver; having the appearance of silver; silvery. 2. (Less common) A variant spelling or prefix relating to Argentina.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "silvery" shine or a high-value, precious metal status. Represents a flow that is polished, bright, and expensive-looking.
