# WORD: Alumines

## Meaning (plain):
Plural form of alumine (more commonly alumina), which is aluminum oxide (Al2O3). It is a tough, hard-wearing material used in ceramics, abrasives, and as a precursor to aluminum metal.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for toughness, hardness, or being a foundational element that can't be easily broken. Refers to a "metallic" or industrial strength in the character or flow.
