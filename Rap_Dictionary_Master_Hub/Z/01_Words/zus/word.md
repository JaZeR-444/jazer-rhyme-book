# WORD: Zus

## Meaning (plain):
An acronym for the Polish state social insurance organization (Zakład Ubezpieczeń Społecznych).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing the "system" or social security; having the insurance/backup for the crew or being tied into the infrastructure.
