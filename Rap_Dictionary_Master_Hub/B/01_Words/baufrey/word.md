# WORD: Baufrey

## Meaning (plain):
An obsolete or archaic term for a beam or a belfry (a tower where bells are hung).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a massive foundational "beam" or a "tower" of sound. Represents someone who stands tall like a belfry and rings out the truth over the whole city.

## Part of speech:
