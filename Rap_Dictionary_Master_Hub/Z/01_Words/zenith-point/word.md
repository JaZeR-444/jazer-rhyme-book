# WORD: Zenith-point

## Meaning (plain):
The specific point directly overhead in the sky; synonym for zenith, representing the ultimate peak or highest location.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing the "exact moment" of peak success; hitting the bullseye at the highest level.

## Syllables:
3 (ze-nith-point)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
zenith, apex, summit, peak

## Antonyms:
nadir, bottom

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: joint, point, anoint / SLANT: limit, spirit / MULTI: Zenith-point high, touching the sky, never say die
