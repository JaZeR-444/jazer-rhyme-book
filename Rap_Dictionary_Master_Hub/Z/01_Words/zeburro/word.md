# WORD: Zeburro

## Meaning (plain):
A hybrid animal resulting from a cross between a zebra and a donkey (also called a zebrasno or zedonk).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
A "rare breed," a mix of wild (zebra) and hard-working (donkey) traits; being a unique hybrid that doesn't fit a mold.

## Syllables:
3 (ze-bur-ro)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
zedonk, zebrasno, zonkey, hybrid

## Antonyms:
None

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: arrow, narrow, sparrow / SLANT: fellow, mellow / MULTI: Zeburro in the pen, start it again, one out of ten

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Wild but I work hard, call me the Zeburro.
2. A rare hybrid, you can't find a Zeburro.
3. Mixing the stripes with the grind, Zeburro.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Rare breed" (Hybrid nature)
2. Wild (Zebra) vs Hard-working/Stubborn (Donkey)
3. One-of-a-kind/Uncommon occurrence
