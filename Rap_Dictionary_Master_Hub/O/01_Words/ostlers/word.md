# WORD: Ostlers

## Meaning (plain):
Plural of ostler; people formerly employed at an inn, hostelry, or stable to care for horses.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the crew members who handle the "horsepower" and the assets of the movement. Represents the loyal team that takes care of the "horses" (the whips and the power) while you're busy running the game. The essential backbone of the stable.

2 (ost-lers)
