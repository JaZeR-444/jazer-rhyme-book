# WORD: Faction

## Meaning (plain):
A small organized dissenting group within a larger one, especially in politics or an organization. Often contentious or self-seeking.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a tight-knit sub-group or a "crew within a crew." Represents a specialized force that has its own agenda and moves independently of the mainstream. Carrying a vibe of rebellion and internal unity.

## Syllables:
2 (fac-tion)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
clique, sector, wing, party, crew

## Antonyms:
whole, unity, total, mainstream

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: action, traction, reaction / SLANT: passion, fashion, fraction / MULTI: faction call, standing tall, never fall

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. My faction is tight, we move in the dark.
2. Contentious energy, faction with the spark.
3. Part of the whole but my faction leave a mark.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Rebel/dissenting group energy
2. "Crew within a crew" unity
3. Internal politics/influence
