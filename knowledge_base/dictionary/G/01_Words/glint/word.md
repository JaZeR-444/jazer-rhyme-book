# WORD: Glint

## Meaning (plain):
A small, quick flash of light; to shine with small, bright flashes. Can also refer to a trace of emotion expressed through the eyes.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a sharp, "shining" strike or a quick flash of brilliance. Represents a moment where the "steel" (talent or literal) catches the light and signals a move.

## Syllables:
1 (glint)

## Part of speech:
noun / verb

## Synonyms:
flash, sparkle, glimmer, shine, twinkle

## Antonyms:
dullness, darkness, shadow

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: sprint, mint, flint / SLANT: hint, tint, print / MULTI: glint of the steel, how I feel, keep it real

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Saw the glint of the chain from a mile away.
2. Flash of brilliance, glint in the play.
3. Quick strike, glint while I say.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Sharp/shining brilliance
2. Visual signal of a move
3. "Steel" or jewelry metaphors

## Tags:
light, common, unique, status
