# WORD: Amoebous

## Meaning (plain):
Resembling or characteristic of an amoeba; especially in being changeable, fluid, or having an indefinite, ever-shifting shape.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a flow that is fluid and unpredictable, changing shape to fit any beat. Represents adaptability and the ability to "morph" into different styles.

## Syllables:
3 (a-moe-bous)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
fluid, changeable, protean, shifting, adaptable

## Antonyms:
rigid, fixed, static, structured

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: focus, notice, bogus / SLANT: lotus, locust, bonus / MULTI: amoebous flow, always on the go, watch the pattern show

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Shape-shifting on the track, flow is amoebous.
2. Unpredictable movement, they can't even notice us.
3. Fluid with the style, keeping it amoebous and wild.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Fluidity/adaptability of flow
2. Unpredictable/shape-shifting nature
3. Breaking out of rigid structures
