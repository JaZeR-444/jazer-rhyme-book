# WORD: Zingaro

## Meaning (plain):
An Italian word for a Romani man; often implies a nomadic lifestyle or someone who cannot stay in one place.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing the "nomad" of the rap game; moving from city to city, stage to stage, without ever being tied down.

## Syllables:
3 (zin-ga-ro)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
nomad, gypsy (sensitive), traveler, wanderer

## Antonyms:
resident, inhabitant

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: arrow, narrow, sparrow / SLANT: shadow, hollow / MULTI: Zingaro mind, one of a kind, leave 'em behind

2. No fixed address, just the rhythm and the beats (Zingaro).
3. Moving through the night, call me Zingaro.
