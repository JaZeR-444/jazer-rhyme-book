# WORD: Victory

## Meaning (plain):
An act of defeating an enemy or opponent in a battle, game, or other competition; a triumph or success.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The ultimate payout of the struggle. Having "victory" means you've crushed the opposition and reached the top of the mountain. Represents the moment when all the work, risk, and strategy result in a total, undeniable win.

## Syllables:
3 (vic-to-ry)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
win, triumph, success, conquest, payout, glory

## Antonyms:
defeat, failure, loss, surrender

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: free, tree, see / SLANT: territory, story, history / MULTI: street victory, purely glory, end of story

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Celebrating the victory, the crown is finally mine.
2. Defeating the enemy, victory in the line.
3. Every verse is a win, victory while I shine.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Crushing the opposition
2. Reaching the "ultimate" success
3. Victory vs. Defeat/Struggle contrast
