# WORD: Zonochlorite

## Meaning (plain):
An obsolete name for impure pumpellyite-Mg, a green mineral occurring in banded structures, often confused with chlorastrolite.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "green" (money) mineral with a banded (layered) structure; having a rare, complex value that others mistake for something else.
