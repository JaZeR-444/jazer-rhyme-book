# WORD: Zadokite

## Meaning (plain):
Relating to Zadok (high priest) or his lineage; often associated with loyalists to the Davidic covenant and the temple.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing loyalty to the "old ways," priestly authority, or being part of an elite, faithful lineage.
