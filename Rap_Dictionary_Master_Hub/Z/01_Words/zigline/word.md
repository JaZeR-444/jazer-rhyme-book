# WORD: Zigline

## Meaning (plain):
A compound of "zig" and "line," referring to a line or path that follows a zigzag pattern with sharp turns.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a non-linear path to success, dodging the direct route, or having a "crooked" but effective style.

## Syllables:
2 (zig-line)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
zigzag, path, course, track

## Antonyms:
straight-line

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: shine, fine, mine, sign / SLANT: design, align / MULTI: Zigline path, avoid the wrath, do the math

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Moving on a Zigline, they can't track the soul.
2. My trajectory is a Zigline, but I reach the goal.
3. Don't look for the straight path, follow the Zigline.

## 3 punchline angles:
