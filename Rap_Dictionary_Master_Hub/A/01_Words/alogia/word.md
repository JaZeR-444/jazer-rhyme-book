# WORD: Alogia

## Meaning (plain):
The inability to speak due to mental deficiency or a psychological condition (such as schizophrenia). It often manifests as poverty of speech or a lack of spontaneous speech.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for silencing the competition or for a moment where the crowd is left speechless. Can also describe a flow so dense it defies normal "speech."

## Syllables:
4 (a-lo-gi-a)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
speechlessness, poverty of speech, silence, mutism

## Antonyms:
verbosity, loquaciousness, eloquence

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: phobia, corneas, utopia / SLANT: soldier, boulder, colder / MULTI: alogia state, left 'em in wait, silence the hate

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Left 'em with alogia, they can't find the words.
2. Silence the room, alogia for the birds.
3. Flow so heavy it induces alogia.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Silencing opponents
2. Crowd being "speechless"
3. Psychological/mental depth metaphors
