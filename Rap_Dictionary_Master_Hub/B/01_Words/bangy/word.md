# WORD: Bangy

## Meaning (plain):
1. (India) A yoke carried on the shoulders for transporting loads. 2. Characterized by banging sounds; clangy. 3. (Slang) Of poor or ghetto quality; or to have sex.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "clangy" or unpolished style that still carries weight (like a yoke). Can also be used to describe something raw and "ghetto" that has an authentic, noisy impact.

## Syllables:
2 (bang-y)

## Part of speech:
adjective / noun

## Synonyms:
clangy, unpolished, raw, yoke, ghetto

## Antonyms:
smooth, refined, quiet, high-end

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: tangy, rangy, slangy / SLANT: candy, brandy, sandy / MULTI: bangy sound, weight on the ground, raw in the town

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Carrying the weight like a bangy on the shoulder.
2. Clangy with the rhythm, keeping it bangy and bolder.
3. Raw and unpolished, pure bangy in the folder.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Carrying a heavy load/burden
2. Noisy/clangy authentic impact
3. "Ghetto" or unpolished realness
