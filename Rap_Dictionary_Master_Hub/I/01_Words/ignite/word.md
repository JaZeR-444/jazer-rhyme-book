# WORD: Ignite

## Meaning (plain):
To catch fire or cause to catch fire; to arouse or inflame (an emotion or situation).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for starting a movement or making a track "explode" with energy. Represents the moment when a raw idea catches fire and spreads throughout the streets. When you ignite the crowd, you're the spark that starts the riot.

## Syllables:
2 (ig-nite)

## Part of speech:
verb

## Synonyms:
spark, inflame, kindle, arouse, explode

## Antonyms:
extinguish, quench, dampen, stifle

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: light, bright, night / SLANT: finite, twilight, highlight / MULTI: ignite the light, end the fight, purely bright

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm here to ignite the scene, watch the sparks fly.
2. Arousing the emotion, ignite 'em while they're high.
3. Every verse is fuel, ignite while I die.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Starting a "fire" or movement
2. Inflaming emotions/situations
3. Being the "spark" of energy

## Tags:
action, common, intensity, energy
