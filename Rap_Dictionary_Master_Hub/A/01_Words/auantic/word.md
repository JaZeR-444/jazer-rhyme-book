# WORD: Authentic

## Meaning (plain):
Of undisputed origin; genuine; not false or imitation. Real and reliable.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The gold standard for any rapper. It means keepin' it 100, being real to the roots, and not faking the lifestyle. It's the opposite of being a "fraud" or "industry plant."

## Syllables:
3 (au-then-tic)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
genuine, real, original, legitimate, true

## Antonyms:
fake, fraudulent, imitation, counterfeit, artificial

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: quick, slick, stick / SLANT: centric, eccentric, frantic / MULTI: authentic flow, watch 'em grow, truth you know

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Everything I spit is authentic, no filter on the truth.
2. I'm keepin' it authentic, from the street to the booth.
3. Undisputed origin, authentic in my youth.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Realness vs. fake industry persona
2. Undisputed legitimacy
3. "Original" status in a world of copies

## Tags:
attitude, status, common
