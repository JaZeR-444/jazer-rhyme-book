# WORD: Navalese

## Meaning (plain):
(Slang/Unique) A specialized language or jargon used by those in the naval branch of service; a dialect characterized by nautical terms and precise technical instructions.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the "insider language" of the culture. Represents a flow that is so thick with street jargon and technical expertise that only the true "sailors" of the game can understand it. Speaking in "navalese" means you're talking over the heads of the general public.
