# WORD: Halt

## Meaning (plain):
To bring or come to an abrupt stop; a suspension of movement or activity. Often used as a military command.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The ultimate command to stop the competition. Represents an authoritative force that can shut down the scene suddenly. When you call a "halt," all other movement ceases.

## Part of speech:
verb / noun

## Synonyms:
stop, cease, pause, desist, suspension

## Antonyms:
