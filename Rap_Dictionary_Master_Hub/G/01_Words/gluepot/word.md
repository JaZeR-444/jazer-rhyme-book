# WORD: Gluepot

## Meaning (plain):
1. A container used for melting and holding glue. 2. (Australian Sports Slang) A very muddy playing field where movement is difficult.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "sticky" situation or a terrain where the competition gets bogged down. Represents an environment that holds people in place, while the master knows how to navigate the "mud."

## Syllables:
2 (glue-pot)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
sticky-situation, mud-field, binder, trap, quagmire

## Antonyms:
clear-path, smooth-surface

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: shot, plot, hot / SLANT: spot, knot, rot / MULTI: gluepot ground, weight on the sound, never be found

2. Muddy in the street, gluepot in the groove.
3. Sticky with the situation, gluepot for the proof.
