# WORD: Zapatero

## Meaning (plain):
A Spanish word for a shoemaker or cobbler; also refers to a shoe rack or a water strider (insect).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing the "hustle" of the craft, someone who "makes the shoes" (the foundations), or a stash of high-end footwear.

## Syllables:
4 (za-pa-te-ro)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
shoemaker, cobbler, shoe rack, artisan

## Antonyms:
None

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: zero, hero, bolero / SLANT: ghetto, meadow / MULTI: Zapatero style, walk a thousand mile, keep the stack on file

## 3 bar-ready examples:

2. Stash in the closet, full Zapatero.
3. Walking on water, call me Zapatero.
