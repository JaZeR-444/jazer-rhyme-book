# WORD: Benty

## Meaning (plain):
1. Characterized by or abounding in bentgrass (coarse, stiff grass). 2. (Informal Slang) Relating to a mental breakdown or being in a state of extreme intoxication (drunk/high).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being "coarse and stiff" (unyielding) or for being in a wild, unhinged state of mind. Represents a raw, gritty energy that is either grounded in the grass or flying high in a "benty" state.

## Syllables:
2 (bent-y)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
coarse, stiff, unhinged, wild, unyielding

## Antonyms:
smooth, sober, calm, flexible

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: plenty, empty, entry / SLANT: twenty, sent me, gently / MULTI: benty state, never late, decide the fate

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Grounded in the street, coarse like a benty field.
2. In a benty state of mind, I never plan to yield.
3. Raw and unhinged, benty while I'm healed.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Unbalanced" or unhinged energy
2. Coarse/stiff resilience (bentgrass)
3. High-intensity state of mind
