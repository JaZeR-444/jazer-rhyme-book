# WORD: Zuurveldt

## Meaning (plain):
A historical region in South Africa (now Albany); characterized by "sour grass" which is unpalatable in winter but good in summer.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing "seasonal" strength; surviving the "sour" times to reach the summer harvest, or being rooted in hard South African soil.
