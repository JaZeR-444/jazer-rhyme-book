# WORD: Fados

## Meaning (plain):
The plural of fado; a Portuguese music genre characterized by mournful tunes and lyrics about the sea, the life of the poor, and a sense of melancholy or resignation.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for "soulful pain" or for telling stories of the struggle with a mournful, heavy intensity. Represents a deep, emotional connection to the "sea" of life and the melancholy of the streets.
