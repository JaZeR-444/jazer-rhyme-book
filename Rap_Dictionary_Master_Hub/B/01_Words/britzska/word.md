# WORD: Britzska

## Meaning (plain):
A type of horse-drawn carriage with four wheels, a long body, and a folding hood over the rear seat. Historically used for travel.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for fast, protected movement or for a "hooded" high-end ride. Represents traveling in style while keeping a part of yourself (the rear seat) under a "hood" or disguised.
