# WORD: Zinger

## Meaning (plain):
A sharp, witty, or pointed remark; something that has vitality or produces startling results.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing that "punchline" that shuts down the room; a witty retort that leaves the opponent speechless.

## Syllables:

## Part of speech:

## Antonyms:
