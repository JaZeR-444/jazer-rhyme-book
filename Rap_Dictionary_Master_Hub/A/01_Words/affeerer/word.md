# WORD: Affeerer

## Meaning (plain):
A person appointed to fix the amount of a fine or amercement when it was not determined by statute (historically in manorial courts).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the judge or the one who decides the cost of a mistake. The one who determines the value or the "penalty" for others' weak flows.

## Syllables:
3 (af-feer-er)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
assessor, judge, appraiser, arbiter

## Antonyms:
N/A

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: clear, near, hear / SLANT: fear, steer, gear / MULTI: affeerer judgment, price of the moment, final assessment

2. Assessing the damage, affeerer of the losses.
3. No fixed price, I'm the affeerer in the court.
