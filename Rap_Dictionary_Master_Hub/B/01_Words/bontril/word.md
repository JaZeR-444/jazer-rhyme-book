# WORD: Bontril

## Meaning (plain):
A brand name for phendimetrazine, a stimulant medication used as an appetite suppressant for the short-term treatment of obesity.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for high-intensity "stimulant" energy and for suppressing any "hunger" that isn't focused on the goal. Represents a sharp, chemical-grade focus that keeps the user moving at a fast pace.
