# WORD: Lumbagos

## Meaning (plain):
An outdated medical term for acute or chronic pain in the lower back (lumbar region).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the "back-breaking" effort required to carry the team or the culture. Represents the literal and metaphorical pain that comes with carrying heavy loads of responsibility or success.

## Syllables:
3 (lum-ba-gos)

## Part of speech:
noun (plural)

## Synonyms:
back-pain, strain, weight, burden, lumbar-ache

## Antonyms:
ease, relief, lightness

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: goes, shows, flows / SLANT: chaos, pathos, halos / MULTI: lumbagos strain, carry the pain, through the rain

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Carrying the whole squad, giving me lumbagos.
2. Back-breaking hustle, while I'm chasing halos.
3. Every win is heavy, lumbagos in the steady.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Carrying the "weight" of others
2. Chronic strain of the hustle
3. Medical/physical metaphors for effort
