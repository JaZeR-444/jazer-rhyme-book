# WORD: Bombyxes

## Meaning (plain):
The plural of Bombyx; a genus of true silk moths, most notably the mulberry silkworm (*Bombyx mori*), whose larvae produce high-quality silk.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a flow that is "silky" and high-end, woven together with precision. Represents someone who transforms raw materials into a valuable, smooth, and luxurious output.

## Syllables:
3 (bom-byx-es)

## Part of speech:
noun (plural)

## Synonyms:
silkworms, silk-moths, weavers, producers
