# WORD: Geile

## Meaning (plain):
(German Slang) Great, cool, awesome, or wicked. Can also mean "hot" or "lusty," and literally refers to fertile soil or luscious vegetation.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for "luscious" or extremely cool intensity. Represents a flow that is highly fertile (productive) and carries a "wicked" or awesome quality that stands out.

## Antonyms:
