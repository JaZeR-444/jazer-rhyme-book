# WORD: Zeme

## Meaning (plain):
In Slavic languages, means "Earth," "Land," or "Ground"; in Albanian, means "Heart."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing the "Earth" (the foundation/soil) and the "Heart" (the core/passion) simultaneously.
