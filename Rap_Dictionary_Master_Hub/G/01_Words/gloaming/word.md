# WORD: Gloaming

## Meaning (plain):
(Poetic) Twilight; the time of day immediately after sunset when the light has mostly faded but it is not yet dark.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the "final hour" or for being the dominant force when the lights are going out for everyone else. Represents a shadowy, poetic period between success and the "darkness" of the unknown.

## Syllables:
2 (gloam-ing)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
twilight, dusk, nightfall, shadow-time

## Antonyms:
dawn, morning, daylight, sunrise

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: roaming, foaming, homing / SLANT: cloning, owning, moaning / MULTI: gloaming light, end the fight, into the night

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Moving through the city in the poetic gloaming.
2. The lights are fading, gloaming for the roaming.
3. Final hour status, gloaming in the lattice.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Dominance in the "shadows"
2. Finality/twilight of a career or era
3. Atmospheric/poetic weight

## Tags:
nature, poetic, unique, movement
