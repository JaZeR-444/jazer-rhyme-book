# WORD: Kansa

## Meaning (plain):
1. A Siouan people of the Kansas river valley (the Kaw people). 2. (Hindu Mythology) A tyrant king of Mathura, known for his power and ultimate defeat by Krishna.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for deep indigenous roots or for an unshakeable, "tyrant-like" dominance over a territory. Represents someone who rules their "valley" with absolute force and has a legendary, historical weight.
