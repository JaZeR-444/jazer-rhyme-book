# WORD: Yo-yo

## Meaning (plain):
Toy on a string; vary fluctuatingly

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Ups and downs, coming back around, treating people like toys

## Syllables:
2 (yo-yo)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
Toy, fluctuate

## Antonyms:
Steady

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: mojo, dojo, logo / SLANT: go / MULTI: slo-mo

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Life like a yo-yo, ups and downs but I spin it
2. Got you on a string like a yo-yo, puppet master
3. Yo-yo diet on the money, fat then skinny

## 3 punchline angles:
1. String control
2. Up and down movement
3. Around the world trick

## Tags:
toy, control, fluctuate, up-down
