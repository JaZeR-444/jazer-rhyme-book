# WORD: Glisten

## Meaning (plain):
To shine by reflecting light from a wet or smooth surface; to give off a lustrous reflection.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for high-end "shine" or for being so polished that you reflect the light from every angle. Represents a status that is smooth, "wet" (expensive), and impossible to ignore.

## Syllables:
2 (glis-ten)

## Part of speech:
verb

## Synonyms:
shine, sparkle, shimmer, glister, flash

## Antonyms:
dull, matt, dim

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: mission, vision, listen / SLANT: piston, mention, condition / MULTI: glisten and shine, polished design, always on time

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Chains glisten under the lights of the city.
2. Polished the flow, glisten and gritty.
3. Lustrous presence, glisten with the pity.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. High-end/expensive shine
2. Polished/finished appearance
3. Reflecting the "light" (attention)

## Tags:
light, status, common, appearance
