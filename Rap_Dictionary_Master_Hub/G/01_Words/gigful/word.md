# WORD: Gigful

## Meaning (plain):
1. The quantity that a gig (a rowboat or carriage) can hold. 2. A full set of performances or songs at a musical engagement (a gig).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "full tank" of energy or a complete, high-intensity set. Represents having everything you need for the performance and bringing a "carriage-full" of heat to the stage.

## Syllables:
2 (gig-ful)

## Part of speech:
noun / adjective

## Synonyms:
full-set, carriage-load, capacity, engagement

## Antonyms:
empty, sparse, partial

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: pull, full, bull / SLANT: blissful, wistful, handful / MULTI: gigful of heat, start the beat, never retreat

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm coming with a gigful, every bar is loaded.
2. Full set energy, gigful and exploded.
3. Capacity reached, gigful while I preached.

## 3 punchline angles:

2. Complete performance/set-list
