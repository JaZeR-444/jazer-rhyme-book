# WORD: Lapidose

## Meaning (plain):
Abounding in stones; of a stony nature; growing in stony ground.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "stony" or unyielding character. Represents someone who thrives in difficult, rocky terrain where others would fail to grow. Carrying a hard, mineral-like intensity.

## Syllables:
3 (la-pi-dose)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
stony, rocky, hard, unyielding, mineral-like

## Antonyms:
soft, sandy, smooth, fertile

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: close, grows, flows / SLANT: focus, notice, bogus / MULTI: lapidose heart, hard from the start, part of the art

2. Thriving in the rocks, lapidose in the close.
3. Stony nature verified, lapidose in the stride.
