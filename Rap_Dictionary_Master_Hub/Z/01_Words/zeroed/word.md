# WORD: Zeroed

## Meaning (plain):
Adjusted to a zero point (e.g., firearm sights); reduced to zero; completely focused on a target.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing "perfect aim," being "zeroed in" on the goal, or having cleared out the competition (reduced to zero).

## Syllables:
2 (ze-roed)

## Part of speech:
Verb (Past Tense) / Adjective

## Synonyms:
aligned, focused, targeted, cleared, erased

## Antonyms:
misaligned, distracted, messy

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: road, code, load, mode / SLANT: flow, slow / MULTI: Zeroed in the sight, ready for the fight, shine in the night

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm zeroed in on the target, I never miss.
2. Competition zeroed, erased from the list.
3. Sights adjusted, yeah I'm fully zeroed.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Zeroed In" (Intense focus/Aim)
2. "Firearm Sights" (Precision metaphors)
3. "Reduced to Zero" (Total elimination)

## Tags:
precision, focused, elimination, action
