# WORD: Lai

## Meaning (plain):
1. In medieval French literature, a lyrical, narrative poem, usually in octosyllabic couplets, often dealing with tales of adventure and romance. 2. A Tibeto-Burman language or people.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a high-end, narrative "lay" or song. Represents a lyrical story that is poetic, structured, and tells a tale of adventure or struggle. A "classic" narrative flow.
