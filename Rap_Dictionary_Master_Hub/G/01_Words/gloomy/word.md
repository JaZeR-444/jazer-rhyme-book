# WORD: Gloomy

## Meaning (plain):
Partially or totally dark; dismally and depressingly dark; low in spirits; melancholy.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a dark, "shadowy" intensity that sets a serious or menacing tone. Represents a mood that is deep, heavy, and doesn't rely on flashy lights to command respect.

## Syllables:
2 (gloo-my)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
dark, somber, dismal, melancholy, shadowy

## Antonyms:
bright, cheerful, sunny, radiant

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: roomy, blooming, tomb / SLANT: doom, plume, loom / MULTI: gloomy state, heavy in the weight, never late

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Setting the mood, keeping it dark and gloomy.
2. In the shadows of the city, gloomy and roomy.
3. Melancholy rhythm, gloomy in the tomb-y.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Dark/menacing atmosphere
2. Melancholy/serious tone
3. Shadows vs. light contrast

## Tags:
emotion, light, common, unique
