# WORD: Zorillos

## Meaning (plain):
Plural of zorillo (diminutive of zorro - fox); refers to skunks or similar small, striped, scent-gland mammals.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a group of "stinking" fakes or a squad of small, defensive predators that you shouldn't mess with.

## Syllables:
3 (zo-ril-los)

## Part of speech:
Noun (Plural)

## Synonyms:
skunks, polecats, small foxes

## Antonyms:
None

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: solo, cocoa, photo / SLANT: motor, rotor / MULTI: Zorillos in the row, watch the garden grow, let 'em know

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. They all smell like fakes, bunch of Zorillos.
2. Don't touch the crew, we moving like Zorillos.
3. Striped and dangerous, counting up the Zorillos.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Stinking Fakes" (Dismissing the competition)
2. "Small but Dangerous"
3. "Zorillos/Solo" (Group vs Individual rhyme)
