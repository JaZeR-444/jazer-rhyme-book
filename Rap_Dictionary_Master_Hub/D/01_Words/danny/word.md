# WORD: Danny

## Meaning (plain):
1. A masculine given name, a diminutive of Daniel, meaning "God is my judge." 2. (British Dialect) A child's term for the hand.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being under a "higher judgment" (only God can judge) or for having "hands-on" control over the situation. Represents a personal, grounded identity that answers to no earthly authority.
