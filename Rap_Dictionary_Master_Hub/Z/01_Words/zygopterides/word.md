# WORD: Zygopterides

## Meaning (plain):
An alternative name for the suborder Zygoptera, which comprises the damselflies.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a group of "delicate but fast" predators; being like a damselfly that is agile in the air and dominant in its territory.

## Syllables:
4 (zy-go-pter-i-des)

## Part of speech:
Noun (Plural)

## Synonyms:
damselflies, odonates, insects, predators

## Antonyms:
None

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: eyes, sighs, ties, lies / SLANT: millions, stallions / MULTI: Zygopterides flight, in the night, out of the sight

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Fast and agile, we the Zygopterides.
2. Predatory insects, spirit of Zygopterides.
3. Flying through the current, call us Zygopterides.

## 3 punchline angles:
