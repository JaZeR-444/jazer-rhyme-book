# WORD: Neighborly

## Meaning (plain):
Characteristic of a good neighbor; friendly and helpful; showing a disposition to live on good terms with those nearby.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for showing "love to the neighborhood" and staying on good terms with the real ones. Represents someone who doesn't forget where they came from and is always ready to be helpful and supportive to their local community. A status of respect and mutual aid.

## Syllables:
3 (neigh-bor-ly)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
friendly, helpful, kind, civil, supportive, local-love

## Antonyms:
hostile, antagonistic, foreign, selfish

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: sky, high, fly / SLANT: overly, properly, sovereignly / MULTI: strictly neighborly, purely properly, end the tragedy

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Staying neighborly with the ones who raised me.
2. Helpful in the hustle, purely neighborly and crazy.
3. Not hostile, just neighborly for the daily.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Showing love" to the community
2. Living on "good terms" with the streets
3. Neighborly vs. Antagonistic contrast

## Tags:
attitude, common, identity, kindness
