# WORD: Kailyard

## Meaning (plain):
1. (Scots) A kitchen garden. 2. A genre of sentimental Scottish literature (late 19th c.) depicting rural life using Scots dialect.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a "home-grown," sentimental, or nostalgic style. Represents someone who keeps it local and roots their story in the familiar "garden" of their origin, often with a specific dialect or flair.
