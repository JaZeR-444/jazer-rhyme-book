# WORD: Zany

## Meaning (plain):
Amusingly unconventional and idiosyncratic; eccentric or absurd in an entertaining way.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Wild, unpredictable, "off the wall" style that keeps the crowd on their toes.

## Syllables:
2 (za-ny)

## Part of speech:
Adjective

## Synonyms:
eccentric, kooky, wild, absurd, comical

## Antonyms:
serious, boring, conventional

END: rainy, brainy, grainy / SLANT: lady, baby / MULTI: Zany with the flow, watch the energy go, let everybody know
