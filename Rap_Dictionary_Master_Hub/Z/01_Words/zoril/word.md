# WORD: Zoril

## Meaning (plain):
A weasel-like African animal (Ictonyx striatus) that resembles a skunk in appearance and habits; known for its bold stripes and defensive scent.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a "striped survivor"; being small but bold and having a "scent" (reputation) that keeps the enemies back.

## Syllables:
2 (zo-ril)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
African skunk, striped polecat, zorilla, predator

## Antonyms:
