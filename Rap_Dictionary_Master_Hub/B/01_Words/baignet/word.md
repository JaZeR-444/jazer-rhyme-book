# WORD: Baignet

## Meaning (plain):
A variant spelling of "beignet," a square, deep-fried pastry sprinkled with powdered sugar, particularly famous in New Orleans.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being "deep-fried" (hardened by heat) and "powdered" (having a polished, sweet-looking exterior). Represents someone who is hard on the inside but appears refined or high-end on the outside.

## Syllables:
2 (baign-et)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
beignet, fritter, pastry, doughnut

N/A
