# WORD: Zanier

## Meaning (plain):
Comparative of zany; more eccentric, absurd, or whimsically comical.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Having an even wilder, more unpredictable style than the rest; out-clowning the jokers.

## Syllables:
3 (za-ni-er)

## Part of speech:
Adjective

## Synonyms:
wilder, crazier, more absurd, more eccentric

## Antonyms:
saner, more serious, more boring

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: barrier, carrier, marrier / SLANT: failure, savior / MULTI: Zanier than before, walking through the door, give 'em some more

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. My flow is zanier, breaking every barrier.
2. They think they're wild, but I'm getting zanier.
3. Cartoon vibes, but the bars are zanier.

1. Being "wilder" or more creative than competition
2. Eccentricity as a strength
3. "Clowning" those who take themselves too seriously
