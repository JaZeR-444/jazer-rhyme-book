# WORD: Headring

## Meaning (plain):
1. A decorated ring worn on the head by married warriors of some African tribes (like the Zulu or Ngoni) as a symbol of maturity and status. 2. A pad worn on the head to facilitate carrying heavy loads.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for reaching a level of "warrior" maturity and being able to carry the heavy weight of the culture. Represents earning your stripes and wearing your responsibility as a crown.
