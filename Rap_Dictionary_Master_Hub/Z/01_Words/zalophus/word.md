# WORD: Zalophus

## Meaning (plain):
A genus of sea lions (e.g., California sea lion); name means "intensive crest," referring to the sagittal crest on males.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing an "intensive crest" or being at the peak/top; agile in the water (the flow) and dominant on the shore.

## Syllables:
3 (za-lo-phus)

## Part of speech:
Noun

## Synonyms:
sea lion, pinniped

## Antonyms:
None

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: thus, bus, fuss / SLANT: focus, lotus / MULTI: Zalophus peak, strong not weak, peak that I seek

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Dominant on the coast like a Zalophus.
2. Intensive crest, high rank, call me Zalophus.
3. Agile in the deep water, spirit of a Zalophus.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. "Intensive crest" (sagittal crest) as a metaphor for a crown or top rank
2. Sea lion's agility vs land-based strength
3. Coastal/Ocean dominance
