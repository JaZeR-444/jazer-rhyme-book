# WORD: Zaddik

## Meaning (plain):
In Judaism, a righteous person of outstanding virtue and piety; the spiritual leader of a Hasidic community.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing holiness, being a "righteous" leader in the community, or having spiritual authority.
