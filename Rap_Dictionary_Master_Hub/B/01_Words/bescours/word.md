# WORD: Bescours

## Meaning (plain):
To scour something thoroughly; to clean, rub, or purge intensely.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for a thorough verbal "scouring" of the scene. Represents a flow that cleans out the fake elements and leaves the track polished and purged of all "rubbish."

## Syllables:
2 (be-scours)

## Part of speech:
verb

## Synonyms:
scour, purge, clean, polish, scrub

## Antonyms:
dirty, clutter, neglect, leave

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: doors, floors, wars / SLANT: yours, stores, pours / MULTI: bescours the floor, open the door, need more

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm here to bescours the game, cleanin' out the trash.
2. Thorough with the bars, bescours for the cash.
3. Every line is polished, bescours with the flash.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Thoroughly "cleaning" the competition
2. Purging the fake elements from the scene
3. Intense refinement/polishing of the work
