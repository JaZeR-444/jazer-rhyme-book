# WORD: Zippers

## Meaning (plain):
Plural of zipper; multiple fasteners for clothes or bags.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing a collection of closed cases, shutting down a group of talkers, or having multiple layers of "clasp" on the stash.

## Syllables:
2 (zip-pers)

## Part of speech:
