# WORD: Facilitate

## Meaning (plain):
To make an action or process easy or easier; to help forward a process or to assist in achieving a goal.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being the "fixer" or the one who makes the moves happen. Represents someone who clears the path, connects the dots, and ensures the whole operation runs smoothly and quickly.

## Syllables:
4 (fa-cil-i-tate)

## Part of speech:
verb

## Synonyms:
assist, ease, help, forward, promote

## Antonyms:
hinder, block, obstruct, delay

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: state, weight, gate / SLANT: inflate, dictate, rotate / MULTI: facilitate the move, watch 'em in the groove, nothing left to prove

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm here to facilitate the growth, clear the whole lane.
2. Making it look easy, facilitate the gain.
3. Facilitate the vision, power in the brain.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Clearing obstacles for the team
2. Making complex moves look easy
3. Being the "fixer" or catalyst

## Tags:
action, common, power, movement
