# WORD: Zizzle

## Meaning (plain):
To make a sputtering or hissing sound (sizzle); informal slang for a cigarette lighter or the sensation of a "hit."

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
The "sizzle" of a hot track; having that "spark" (lighter) that ignites the room.

## Syllables:
2 (ziz-zle)

## Part of speech:
Noun/Verb

## Synonyms:
sizzle, hiss, spark, lighter, heat

## Antonyms:
freeze, chill

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: sizzle, drizzle, fizzle / SLANT: little, brittle / MULTI: Zizzle in the heat, making it complete, best on the street

3. Don't let it fizzle, keep up the Zizzle.
