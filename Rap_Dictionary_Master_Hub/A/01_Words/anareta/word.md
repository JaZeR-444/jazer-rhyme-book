# WORD: Anareta

## Meaning (plain):
(Astrology) The planet that destroys life; the planet that, in its aspects, is considered to be the "killer" or indicator of the end of life.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for an unstoppable closer or a lethal force on the track. Refers to someone who ends careers or terminates the competition with planetary weight.

## Syllables:
4 (an-a-re-ta)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
killer planet, destroyer, terminator, nemesis

## Antonyms:
Hyleg (giver of life)

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: data, beta, theta / SLANT: crater, hater, greater / MULTI: anareta strike, end the night, cosmic might

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm the anareta, coming to end the whole show.
2. Cosmic destruction, anareta with the blow.
3. Your career hit an aspect, anareta in the flow.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Career-ending/lethal energy
2. Cosmic/astrological weight
3. Finality/termination of a rival
