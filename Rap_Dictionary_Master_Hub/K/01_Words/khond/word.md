# WORD: Khond

## Meaning (plain):
An indigenous Adivasi tribal community in India, traditionally hunter-gatherers living in the mountain regions of Odisha.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for deep mountain resilience and a primal, "hunter-gatherer" work ethic. Represents a connection to the untamed, high-altitude places and a spirit that survives where others can't.
