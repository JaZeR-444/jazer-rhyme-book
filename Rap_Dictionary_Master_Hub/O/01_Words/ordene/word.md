# WORD: Ordene

## Meaning (plain):
1. (Anglo-Norman) Order, sequence, or a grade/rank within a hierarchy. 2. (Spanish) Conjugation of "ordenar" (to order, arrange, command, or ordain).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for your "rank" or command level in the game. Represents someone who has the power to "order" the scene and who sits at a high "grade" in the street hierarchy. Carrying a status that is both organized and authoritative.
