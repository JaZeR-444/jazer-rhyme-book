# WORD: Zoophobe

## Meaning (plain):
A person who has a morbid or irrational fear of animals.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Dismissing an opponent who is "scared of the beasts"; someone who can't handle the raw animalistic energy of the real ones.
