# WORD: Jaw

## Meaning (plain):
1. Either of the two bony or cartilaginous structures forming the framework of the mouth. 2. (Informal) To talk at length; to chat or gossip.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for the literal source of the bars. Having a "strong jaw" means being able to deliver heavy hits, while "jawing" refers to the continuous, rhythmic output of words. Represents the framework of the verbal attack.
