# WORD: Neck

## Meaning (plain):
The part of an animal or human that connects the head with the body; a narrow part of an object (like a bottle or guitar).

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for status ("head and shoulders" above) or for high-risk situations where your reputation is "on the line." Represents the connection between the "brain" (the ideas) and the "body" (the action). Also refers to wearing heavy jewelry (iced-out neck).
