# WORD: Zigzag

## Meaning (plain):
A line or course characterized by sharp turns alternating from side to side; to move in such a pattern.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Representing an unpredictable flow, weaving through the competition, or dodging the traps set by haters.

## Syllables:
2 (zig-zag)

## Part of speech:
Noun/Verb/Adjective

## Synonyms:
weave, swerve, dodge, twist, turn

## Antonyms:
straight, direct

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: flag, brag, drag, tag / SLANT: bag, sag / MULTI: Zigzag flow, watch the garden grow, let 'em know

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I weaving through the traffic, doing the Zigzag.
2. Unpredictable movement, flow like a Zigzag.
3. They try to pin me down, but I move in a Zigzag.
