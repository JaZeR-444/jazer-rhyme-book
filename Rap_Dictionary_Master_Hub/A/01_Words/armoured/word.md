# WORD: Armoured

## Meaning (plain):
Protected by a tough or metal covering (armor); specifically used for military vehicles, personnel, or animals with a natural protective shell.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for being untouchable, bulletproof, or having a heavy emotional/defensive exterior. Represents high-level security and being prepared for any "battle."

## Syllables:
2 (ar-moured)

## Part of speech:
adjective

## Synonyms:
protected, reinforced, shielded, bulletproof, tank-like

## Antonyms:
vulnerable, exposed, naked, weak

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: board, sword, lord / SLANT: toward, accord, afford / MULTI: armoured car, raise the bar, from afar

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. I'm armoured up, the arrows never penetrate.
2. Bulletproof spirit, armoured in the state.
3. Reinforced rhythm, armoured with the weight.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Untouchable/bulletproof status
2. Defensive strength/security
3. Military/heavy equipment metaphors
