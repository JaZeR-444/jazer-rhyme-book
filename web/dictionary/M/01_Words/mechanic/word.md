# WORD: Mechanic

## Meaning (plain):
A skilled tradesperson who uses tools to build, maintain, or repair machinery, especially engines.

## Rap meaning (how I'd say it):
Metaphor for someone who "fixes" or builds the game from the ground up. Represents a high-level technical skill where you know how the "engine" of a track works and how to tune it for maximum performance. A master of the internal machinery of rap.

## Syllables:
3 (me-chan-ic)

## Part of speech:
noun

## Synonyms:
fixer, engineer, technician, grease-monkey, builder

## Antonyms:
destroyer, amateur, breaker

## Rhyme ideas (end / slant / multi):
END: panic, titanic, volcanic / SLANT: organic, satanic, frantic / MULTI: street mechanic, don't panic, move titanic

## 3 bar-ready examples:
1. Tuning up the rhythm, call me the street mechanic.
2. Built the engine myself, purely titanic.
3. Don't panic, I'm the mechanic in the dynamic.

## 3 punchline angles:
1. Fixing/tuning the "engine" of the game
2. Technical mastery of the craft
3. Working under the hood while others just drive
